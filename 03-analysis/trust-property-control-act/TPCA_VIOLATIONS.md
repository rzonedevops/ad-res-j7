**Provision**: Trustees must exercise care, skill, and diligence of a reasonably prudent person in managing trust affairs.

|-----------|---------|
| **Violator** | Daniel Jacobus Bantjies (Trustee and Trust Accountant) |

### Application to Daniel Jacobus Bantjies

### Joint and Several Liability

Where multiple trustees breach duties in concert, they may be held jointly and severally liable. Peter and Bantjies acted in concert to obtain the interdict (Peter's affidavit + Bantjies' confirmatory affidavit + Bantjies acting as Commissioner of Oaths).
