## 3. Detailed Paragraph Analysis

### 3.1. AD Paragraphs 10.5 - 10.10.23: Systematic Financial Misconduct

*   **Peter's Claim**: Peter alleges systematic financial misconduct by Daniel, citing unexplained IT expenses, an unauthorized R500K payment, and a lack of proper documentation.

*   **Proven Truth & Evidence**: The claim is a gross misrepresentation of the facts, constituting **Material Non-Disclosure** and **Perjury with Foreknowledge**. The evidence in `1_critical.md` and its supporting documents (`RWD_REVENUE_INTEGRITY_ANALYSIS.md`) shows:
    *   **Legal Impossibility of Revenue**: RWD had no stock, no inventory, and no assets to sell. It had no independent capacity to generate revenue. All sales were conducted on a Shopify platform owned and paid for by Daniel's UK entity, RegimA Zone Ltd.
    *   **Unjust Enrichment**: RWD appropriated all revenue from sales on Daniel's platform without ever compensating the platform owner for its use (a potential claim of R2.94M - R6.88M).
    *   **Concealment of Facts**: Peter, as a director, had full knowledge of this operational structure. His affidavit deliberately conceals this reality, instead fabricating a narrative of financial abuse by focusing only on the expenses, not the fraudulent revenue model he orchestrated.

### 3.2. AD Paragraph 10.5.2: Placing Respondents under Probation
