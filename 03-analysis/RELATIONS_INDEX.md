## Integrated Arena-Agent Mapping

### Case 1: Civil Response

#### Ground 1 - Bad Faith / Clean Hands

**Arena (Legal Principles)**:
- `bona-fides` - good faith requirement violated
- `fraus-omnia-corrumpit` - fraud corrupts everything
- `nemo-iudex-in-causa-sua` - no judge in own cause (Peter created problems)
- Clean hands doctrine - bars equitable relief when applicant unclean
- lex/civ-proc/ - ex parte application standards

**Agent (Evidence)**:
- JF01 (Shopify email) - proves Peter aware of independent operations
- JF07 (System access) - proves Peter created documentation gap
- JF09 (Timeline) - proves causation: card cancellations → problems → interdict
- JF10 (Director loans) - proves hypocrisy in financial claims
- SF2 (Sage screenshots) - proves Rynette's system control

**Relation**: Peter deliberately created the problems he complains about (card cancellations, system restrictions), violating good faith and clean hands principles. Evidence shows he knew about independent operations (JF01), took actions that created documentation gaps (JF07, JF09), then used those gaps to justify interdict - classic bad faith.

**Strength**: ⭐⭐⭐ STRONG - Direct causation chain, contemporaneous evidence, contradicts Peter's narrative

---

#### Ground 2 - Material Non-Disclosure

**Arena (Legal Principles)**:
- `uberrima-fides` - utmost good faith required in ex parte applications
- `audi-alteram-partem` - duty to present other side's case
- `ei-qui-affirmat-incumbit-probatio` - burden on claimant to disclose all material facts
- lex/civ-proc/ - ex parte disclosure duties
- Material non-disclosure makes interdict void ab initio

**Agent (Evidence)**:
- JF04 (Responsible Person) - 37 jurisdictions where Jax is RP (not disclosed)
- JF05 (Regulatory compliance) - compliance obligations (not disclosed)
- JF06 (Disproportionality) - R68.1M+ harm vs R500K alleged (not disclosed)
- JF09 (Timeline) - Peter's own conduct creating problems (not disclosed)

**Relation**: Peter had duty to disclose all material facts that might affect Court's decision. He failed to disclose: (1) Jax's critical role as Responsible Person in 37 jurisdictions, (2) that interdict would cause R68M+ harm vs R500K he alleged, (3) that his own actions created the problems he complained about. These omissions were material and deliberate, vitiating the interdict.

**Strength**: ⭐⭐⭐ STRONG - Clear ex parte duty violations, material omissions, legal basis for rescission

---

#### Ground 3 - Gross Disproportionality

**Arena (Legal Principles)**:
- Constitutional proportionality (Section 36)
- `de-minimis-non-curat-lex` - law doesn't concern trifles (inverted: relief causes greater harm)
- lex/civ/ - proportionality in remedies
- Equitable relief must not cause greater harm than it prevents

**Agent (Evidence)**:
- JF06 (Disproportionality analysis) - R68.141M+ harm vs R500K alleged
- JF07 (System access impact) - business operations completely disrupted
- JF03 (Financial records) - quantified losses
- JF04 (Responsible Person) - regulatory compliance jeopardized
- evidence/shopify_reports/ - revenue impact documentation

**Relation**: Peter alleged R500K potential harm (reversible). Interdict caused R68.141M+ actual harm (largely irreversible) - 136:1 ratio. This gross disproportionality violates constitutional proportionality principle. Relief causes 136 times more harm than it prevents, which is legally impermissible.

**Strength**: ⭐⭐⭐ STRONG - Massive harm ratio, well-documented, constitutional basis

### Case 2: Criminal Case

#### Perjury (Peter Faucitt & Daniel Bantjes)

**Arena (Legal Principles)**:
- `actus-non-facit-reum-nisi-mens-sit-rea` - guilty mind required
- `nullum-crimen-sine-lege` - crime defined by statute
- Criminal Procedure Act 51 of 1977, Section 319 (perjury)
- lex/cri/ - perjury elements: false statement, material to proceedings, knowledge of falsity, made under oath
- lex/evid/ - proof beyond reasonable doubt

**Agent (Evidence - Currently Available)**:
- JF01 (Shopify email) - contradicts Peter's claims about operations
- JF09 (Timeline) - contradicts sworn statements about sequence
- case_2025_137857/01_court_documents/ - Peter's and Bantjes' affidavits

**Agent (Evidence - Post-Interdict Required)**:
- Email archives (10,000+ emails, 2017-2025) - direct contradictions
- System access logs - proof of knowledge
- Communication records - demonstrates intent

**Relation**: Peter and Bantjes made false statements under oath (affidavits) that are contradicted by documentary evidence (JF01, JF09). Post-interdict access will provide email evidence showing they knew statements were false when made. This satisfies all perjury elements: false statements, material to proceedings, knowledge of falsity, made under oath.

**Strength**: ⭐⭐⭐ STRONG (post-interdict) - Current evidence shows contradictions, full evidence will prove knowledge and intent
