## Section 10: Delinquency Allegations (AD 10.1 - 10.13)

| AD Paragraph | AD Content (Summary) | JR (Jacqueline's Response) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **10.1** | Seeks order declaring respondents delinquent or under probation. | The contents of this paragraph are noted. The relief sought is draconian and entirely disproportionate to any conduct on my part. This application is not a genuine attempt to protect the corporate respondents but is rather a punitive measure to oust me from the businesses I have helped build over decades. |
| **10.2** | Claims conduct justifies urgent relief under PART A. | The allegations are denied. The Applicant fails to establish any conduct on my part that would justify the extraordinary and urgent relief sought. The true urgency stems from the Applicant's attempts to conceal the financial irregularities that I and the Second Respondent have sought to expose. |
| **10.3** | Alleges conduct is "clearly and evidently" detrimental. | The allegation is denied. My conduct has at all times been directed at protecting the interests of the corporate respondents. The Applicant's assertion is a gross mischaracterization of my efforts to uphold good corporate governance in the face of significant financial mismanagement by others. |
| **10.4** | Alleges conduct contravenes Companies Act and CC Act. | This is a bare and unsubstantiated legal conclusion, and it is denied. The Applicant fails to specify any act or omission that constitutes a contravention of the cited legislation. I have at all times been mindful of my fiduciary duties. |
| **10.5 - 10.5.2** | Cites legal grounds for delinquency/probation. | The contents of these paragraphs are noted as the Applicant's interpretation of the law. The Applicant has failed to establish the factual predicate required to invoke these sections of the Companies Act. This application is an abuse of the court's process. |
| **10.6 - 10.6.3** | Cites fiduciary duties under the Close Corporations Act. | The contents of these paragraphs are noted as a recitation of the law. I am fully aware of my fiduciary duties and have consistently acted in accordance therewith. The Applicant's selective quoting of the law is a prelude to baseless allegations. |
| **10.7 - 10.7.1.2** | Cites liability for breach of fiduciary duty. | The contents of these paragraphs are noted as a recitation of the law. I deny any breach of my fiduciary duties and therefore deny any liability for any alleged losses or damages. |
| **10.8** | Cites disqualification for misconduct. | The contents of this paragraph are noted as a recitation of the law. I deny any misconduct that would warrant my disqualification from management. |
| **10.9 - 10.9.3.3** | Cites grounds for delinquency (gross abuse, harm, negligence, fraud). | I deny each of these baseless allegations. The Applicant provides no evidence to support these serious accusations. To the contrary, I have acted to *prevent* harm. For example, on 15 May 2025, I confronted a co-director, Rynette, about ZAR 1,035,000 owed by RegimA Skin Treatments to another entity, Rezonance, which I believed was an improper retention of funds. This was an act of responsible oversight, not abuse. |
| **10.10 - 10.10.2.3**| Cites grounds for probation (inconsistent duties, oppressive conduct). | I deny these allegations. My actions have not been inconsistent with my duties, nor have they been oppressive or prejudicial to the Applicant. The Applicant's feeling of being disregarded stems from my refusal to acquiesce in conduct that I believe is detrimental to the companies. The Applicant's own conduct, such as including me in an interdict on 13 August 2025, a mere two days after I signed a document backdating his designation as "Main Trustee," is the true example of oppressive and prejudicial conduct. |
| **10.11** | Concludes that he is advised to seek a delinquency order. | The Applicant's conclusion is noted. It is, however, based on a foundation of false premises and unsubstantiated allegations, and I am advised that it is without legal merit. |
| **10.12** | States full extent of transgressions is unknown and requests a forensic investigation. | I agree that the full extent of financial transgressions needs to be determined. However, I emphatically deny that these are *my* transgressions. I welcome a truly independent forensic investigation, as it will expose the financial misconduct I have been trying to bring to light, including the R5.4 million "stock adjustment" in Strategic Logistics (SLG) and the two years of unallocated expenses managed by Rynette while using the Applicant's own email address. |
| **10.13** | Claims "significant sums of money...cannot be accounted for." | I agree that significant sums cannot be accounted for. This is precisely the issue I have raised internally. The Applicant incorrectly attributes this to me and my son, when the evidence points to a systematic pattern of revenue hijacking and expense dumping orchestrated by others, which the Applicant has either ignored or enabled. |
