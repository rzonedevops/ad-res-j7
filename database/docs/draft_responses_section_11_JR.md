## Section 11: UK Operations (AD 11.1 - 11.9)

| AD Paragraph | AD Content (Summary) | JR (Jacqueline's Response) |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **11.1** | We began companies in the UK, which my son has been appointed to direct. | Admitted. |
| **11.2** | These companies fall under the UK head office, RegimA UK Ltd. | This is denied. The Applicant misunderstands the corporate structure. RegimA UK Ltd is one of several UK entities. My son's company, RegimA Zone Ltd, is a separate entity and is not a subsidiary of RegimA UK Ltd. |
| **11.3** | My wife, my son, and I are the directors thereof. | This is admitted in respect of RegimA UK Ltd. However, the Applicant is not a director of RegimA Zone Ltd. |
| **11.4** | My involvement has been minimal. | The contents are noted. |
| **11.5** | My son was appointed to oversee the UK branch. | This is admitted. My son was appointed to rescue the UK operations after the massive fraud perpetrated by the previous managing director, Isaac Chesno. |
| **11.6** | The SA companies ship to the UK but do not receive payment, due to past fraud. | The contents are noted. The financial recovery of the UK operations after the Chesno fraud has been a long and arduous process, managed entirely by my son. |
| **11.7** | The fraud was perpetrated by Isaac Chesno, which resulted in our son's appointment. | Admitted. The Applicant correctly states that my son's appointment was a direct result of the half-a-million-pound fraud committed by Isaac Chesno. |
| **11.8** | I fear the continued non-payment may be a ruse. | This is a baseless and offensive insinuation. The "continued non-payment" is a reflection of the long-term damage caused by the Chesno fraud, which my son has worked tirelessly for 8 years to rectify. To suggest this is a "ruse" is a gross insult to my son's efforts. |
| **11.9** | I have no idea about the financial wellness of the UK branches and intend to approach the UK Court. | The Applicant's professed ignorance is of his own making. He has had every opportunity to be involved and informed. His threat to approach the UK Court is another example of his campaign of harassment against my son. |
