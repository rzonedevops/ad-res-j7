# TRANSITION COMPLETION STRATEGY
## Completing the Planned ZA Shopify Store Transition

### EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The transition completion strategy focuses on completing the planned transition from UK stores to ZA stores, including the restoration of the 2 ZA Shopify stores that were set up and linked to account systems, and completing the Dermal B2B account setup that was nearly there before the attack. This strategy ensures POPIA compliance and respects client consent throughout the transition process.

---

## THE PLANNED TRANSITION

### ORIGINAL TRANSITION PLAN

**PARAGRAPH PLAN-1.1**: The original transition plan involved:

- **2 ZA Shopify Stores**: Set up and linked to account systems
- **Client Migration**: Gradually adding clients from UK store to ZA stores
- **POPIA Compliance**: Based on clients' own POPIA consent
- **B2B Accounts**: Clients managing their own PII and orders

**PARAGRAPH PLAN-1.2**: This plan was designed to ensure a smooth transition while maintaining POPIA compliance and respecting client choice.

**PARAGRAPH PLAN-1.3**: The transition was part of the legitimate business strategy to create a protective environment for staff and clients.

### PRE-ATTACK STATUS

**PARAGRAPH STATUS-1.1**: Before the attack, the transition was well underway:

- **ZA Shopify Stores**: 2 stores were set up and linked to account systems
- **Dermal B2B**: One distributor (Dermal) was nearly there setting up clients with B2B accounts
- **PII Management**: Clients were managing their own PII and orders
- **POPIA Compliance**: The setup was POPIA compliant

**PARAGRAPH STATUS-1.2**: The transition was progressing smoothly and was nearly complete when the attack occurred.

**PARAGRAPH STATUS-1.3**: The attack disrupted this legitimate business transition and must be addressed.
