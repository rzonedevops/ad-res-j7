   - Paragraph number

### Step 7.1: Decide on Supplementary vs. Amended Affidavit

**Task:** Determine the best procedural approach

**Actions:**
1. **Consult with legal counsel** on procedural options:
   - File a supplementary affidavit (adds to existing affidavit)
   - File an amended affidavit (replaces existing affidavit)
   - File both (supplementary for new evidence, amended for corrections)

2. **Consider the advantages and disadvantages:**
   - **Supplementary:** Doesn't look like backtracking, but may seem defensive
   - **Amended:** Comprehensive and clean, but may look like we made mistakes
   - **Both:** Most thorough, but may be procedurally complex

3. **Check the court rules:**
   - What does the Uniform Rules of Court say?
   - Do we need leave of court to amend?
   - What are the time limits?

4. **Make a decision** based on counsel's advice

**Deliverable:**
