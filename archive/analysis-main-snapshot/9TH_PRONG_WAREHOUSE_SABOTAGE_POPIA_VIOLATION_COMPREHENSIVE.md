## THE POPIA VIOLATIONS

### Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA) Requirements

**Section 9: Processing Limitation**
- Personal information must be processed **lawfully** and in a **reasonable manner**
- Must not infringe the **privacy** of the data subject

**Section 11: Purpose Specification**
- Personal information must be collected for a **specific, explicitly defined and lawful purpose**
- Must not be processed in a way that is **incompatible** with that purpose

**Section 14: Further Processing Limitation**
- Further processing must be **compatible** with the purpose of collection
- Extracting customer data for orders but **NOT FULFILLING** = **INCOMPATIBLE PURPOSE**

### The POPIA Violations

**Violation #1: Unlawful Processing**
- Customer data collected under pretense of order fulfilment
- **NO FULFILMENT** = **UNLAWFUL PROCESSING**

**Violation #2: Purpose Incompatibility**
- Purpose: Fulfil customer orders
- Reality: Extract data, charge customers, **NO FULFILMENT**
- **INCOMPATIBLE PURPOSE** = **POPIA VIOLATION**

**Violation #3: Privacy Infringement**
- Customers' personal information extracted without their knowledge
- Used for purposes **OTHER THAN** order fulfilment
- **PRIVACY INFRINGEMENT** = **POPIA VIOLATION**
