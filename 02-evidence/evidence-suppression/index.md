# Evidence Suppression Master Index

This folder contains all documents, transcriptions, commentary, and insights related to the **suppression of evidence** in case 2025-137857. The evidence demonstrates a coordinated effort to conceal financial irregularities, obstruct justice, and mislead the court.

## Key Themes

1.  **Concealment of Financial Crimes:** The settlement agreements (AF2) were designed to prevent the reporting of financial crimes and suppress evidence of irregularities.
2.  **Obstruction of Justice:** Rynette Farrar's perjury complaint (SF15) is a direct attempt to intimidate a witness and suppress truthful evidence.
3.  **Misleading the Court:** The urgent application (KF0019) contains numerous allegations that are contradicted by the very evidence submitted.
4.  **Coordinated Legal Action:** The timing and content of legal actions suggest a coordinated strategy to protect individuals from liability.
