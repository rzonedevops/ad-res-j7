## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

Daniel Faucitt's UK company **RegimA Zone Ltd** owns and pays for the Shopify e-commerce platforms used by South African entities **RegimA Worldwide Distribution (RWW)** and **RegimA SA**. Over the 12-month period from September 2024 to August 2025, these platforms generated **R34,951,765.30** in total sales.

Peter Faucitt and related entities have used these platforms without compensation to Daniel's UK company, constituting **unjust enrichment**. The platforms were also subject to interference (orders removed May 22, 2025) and domain diversion (regimaskin.co.za registered by Adderory on May 29, 2025).
