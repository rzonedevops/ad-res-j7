## CRITICAL REVELATION

All witness statements must link to the fundamental revelation:

**Dan & Kay Shopify Platform Payment Structure:**
- The Shopify e-commerce platform was **owned and paid for** by Dan's UK company **RegimA Zone Ltd**
- Dan & Kayla built this platform independently from 2016-2019
- **RegimA Worldwide Distribution (RWD) ZA** operated on this platform **without paying** the platform owner
- **RWD ZA has no independent revenue stream** of its own
- All revenue generated through the platform belongs to the platform owner (RegimA Zone Ltd)

---

## LEGAL SIGNIFICANCE

This revelation establishes:

1. **Platform Ownership:** Dan's UK company owns the Shopify infrastructure
2. **Payment Evidence:** RegimA Zone Ltd paid R140,000-R280,000+ for platform over 28 months
3. **Unjust Enrichment:** RWD ZA benefited from platform without compensation
4. **Revenue Stream Control:** RWD ZA's revenue is dependent on Dan's platform
5. **Independent Operations:** Dan & Kayla operated independently since 2016
