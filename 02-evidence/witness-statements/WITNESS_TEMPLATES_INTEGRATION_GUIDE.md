## CORE REVELATION

### The Fundamental Discovery

All witness statements must establish and reinforce:

1. **Platform Ownership:** The Shopify e-commerce platform is owned by RegimA Zone Ltd (Daniel's UK company)
2. **Platform Payment:** RegimA Zone Ltd paid R140,000-R280,000+ for platform infrastructure
3. **Unauthorized Usage:** RWD ZA operated on this platform without payment
4. **Revenue Dependency:** RWD ZA has no independent revenue stream of its own
5. **Unjust Enrichment:** RWD ZA benefited from infrastructure it did not pay for

### Legal Significance

This revelation establishes:
- Unjust enrichment cause of action
- Platform ownership rights
- Intellectual property claims
- Damages quantification basis
- Counter-claims to applicant's interdict

## LEGAL FRAMEWORK INTEGRATION

### Unjust Enrichment Elements

Each witness statement addresses these elements:

**1. Enrichment**
- RWD ZA benefited from platform usage
- Value received: e-commerce capability, revenue generation

**2. Impoverishment**
- RegimA Zone Ltd bore all platform costs
- Amount: R140,000-R280,000+ (minimum)

**3. Causation**
- RWD ZA's enrichment directly caused RegimA Zone Ltd's impoverishment
- One-to-one relationship between platform usage and costs

**4. Absence of Legal Ground**
- No contract or agreement for platform usage
- No payment arrangement
- No license or permission

**5. Contra Bonos Mores**
- Unjust for RWD ZA to retain benefit without payment
- Industry standards require platform usage fees
- Moral and legal obligation to compensate platform owner

## COMMISSIONER OF OATHS REQUIREMENTS

### South African Legal Requirements

**Each witness statement must:**
- Be signed before a Commissioner of Oaths
- Include commissioner's full details
- Be dated and place-stamped
- Include witness's oath/affirmation

**Commissioner Details Required:**
- Full name
- Designation/title
- Physical address
- Date of commissioning
- Signature and stamp

**Process:**
1. Complete all template fields
2. Review with witness
3. Arrange commissioning appointment
4. Witness signs in presence of commissioner
5. Commissioner verifies identity
6. Commissioner completes certification section
7. Original retained, copies made for court
