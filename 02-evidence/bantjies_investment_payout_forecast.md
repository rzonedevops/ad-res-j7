## 15. Call Option

### 15.1 Call Option Entitlement

The Company shall be entitled, during the Option Period, to acquire the Option Shares from the A Ordinary Shareholder (the **"Call Option"**) by giving written notice to the A Ordinary Shareholder requiring the A Ordinary Shareholder to sell the Option Shares to the Company (the **"Call Notice"**), in which event, the A Ordinary Shareholder shall be obliged to sell to the Company the Option Shares on the following terms and conditions:

#### 15.1.1 Call Option Exercise Closing Date

the A Ordinary Shareholder shall be obliged to sell the Option Shares on the 3rd business day following receipt of the Call Notice from the Company (the **"Call Option Exercise Closing Date"**);

#### 15.1.2 Purchase Price

the purchase price of the Option Shares shall be as follows:

##### 15.1.2.1 First Year of Option Period

**Period:** MAY 2016 - APR 2017  
**Total Amount:** R18 685 000  
**Price per Option Share:** R3,737

##### 15.1.2.2 Second Year of Option Period

**Period:** MAY 2017 - APR 2027  
**Total Amount:** R23 165 000  
**Price per Option Share:** R4,633

##### 15.1.2.3 Final Year of Option Period

**Period:** MAY 2029 - APR 2029 *(Note: This date range as transcribed appears inconsistent - may be OCR error or document typo)*  
**Total Amount:** R28 730 000  
**Price per Option Share:** R5,746

(the **"Option Purchase Price"**).

#### 15.1.3 Payment Terms

the Option Purchase Price shall be payable on the Call Option Exercise Closing Date, against compliance by the A Ordinary Shareholder with its obligations in clause 15.1.4.1 and compliance by the Company with its obligations in clause 15.1.4.2 below;

#### 15.1.4 Closing Meeting

on the Call Option Exercise Closing Date, representatives of the Parties shall meet at 10h00 at such place as the Parties may agree, at which:

##### 15.1.4.1 A Ordinary Shareholder's Obligations

the A Ordinary Shareholder shall deliver to the Company:

###### 15.1.4.1.1 Share Certificates
