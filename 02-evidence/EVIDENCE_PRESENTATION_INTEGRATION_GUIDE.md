## THE CENTRAL REVELATION

### Core Discovery

**Dan & Kay's Shopify platform (owned by RegimA Zone Ltd UK) was paid for by this UK company, while RWD ZA generated R2.94M-R6.88M revenue using this platform without compensation and has no independent revenue stream of its own.**

### Five Core Facts

1. **RegimA Zone Ltd (UK company owned by Dan & Kay) owns the Shopify platform**
   - Evidence: JF-DAN-SYSTEM5 (Platform ownership documentation)

2. **RegimA Zone Ltd paid R140,000 - R280,000 for the platform over 28 months**
   - Evidence: JF-DAN-SYSTEM6 (Platform invoices)

3. **RWD ZA generated R2.94M - R6.88M revenue on this platform**
   - Evidence: JF-DAN-SYSTEM7 (RWD revenue reports)

4. **RWD ZA never paid RegimA Zone Ltd for platform usage (R 0.00 compensation)**
   - Evidence: JF-BS1 (Bank statements showing no payments)

5. **RWD ZA has no independent revenue stream - 100% dependent on Dan & Kay's platform**
   - Evidence: JF-DAN-SYSTEM1-4 (System documentation showing dependency)
