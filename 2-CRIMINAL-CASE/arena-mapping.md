## Charges Against Peter Faucitt

### 1. Perjury

**Statute**: Criminal Procedure Act 51 of 1977, Section 319

**Elements Required**:
1. Statement made under oath
2. Statement is false
3. Material to the proceedings
4. Knowledge of falsity (mens rea)

**Legal Principles**:
- `actus-non-facit-reum-nisi-mens-sit-rea` (guilty mind required)
- `nullum-crimen-sine-lege` (crime defined by statute)
- `lex/cri/` - Perjury provisions

**Application**: Peter's founding affidavit contains false statements contradicted by documentary evidence, made under oath, material to interdict application.

**Strength**: ⭐⭐⭐ STRONG (post-interdict access)

---

### 2. Fraud (Common Law)

**Elements Required**:
1. Misrepresentation
2. Knowledge of falsity (scienter)
3. Intent to defraud
4. Actual prejudice

**Legal Principles**:
- `fraus-omnia-corrumpit` (fraud corrupts everything)
- `actus-non-facit-reum-nisi-mens-sit-rea` (intent required)
- Common law fraud provisions
- `lex/cri/` - Fraud framework

**Application**: Interdict obtained through misrepresentation, Peter knew statements false, intended to cause harm, actual prejudice R68M+.

**Strength**: ⭐⭐⭐ STRONG (post-interdict access)

---

### 3. Theft

**Statute**: Criminal Law Amendment Act 1 of 1988

**Elements Required**:
1. Appropriation
2. Property belonging to another
3. Intent to permanently deprive
4. Without consent

**Legal Principles**:
- `nemo-dat-quod-non-habet` (cannot give what you don't have)
- `lex/cri/` - Theft provisions
- Revenue hijacking as theft

**Application**: Revenue from platform owned by Dan's UK company diverted to Peter's entities without consent.

**Strength**: ⭐⭐ MEDIUM (requires full financial evidence post-interdict)

---

### 4. Obstruction of Justice

**Statute**: Criminal Procedure Act 51 of 1977

**Elements Required**:
1. Deliberate interference with justice administration
2. Intent to obstruct
3. Actual obstruction

**Legal Principles**:
- `fraus-omnia-corrumpit` (deliberate obstruction)
- `lex/cri/` - Obstruction provisions

**Application**: Card cancellations to create documentation gap, system restrictions to prevent evidence gathering.

**Strength**: ⭐⭐⭐ STRONG (timing and pattern clear)
