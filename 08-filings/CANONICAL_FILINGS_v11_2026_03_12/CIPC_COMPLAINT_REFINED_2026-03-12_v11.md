This complaint details severe, systematic violations of the Companies Act, 2008 by the respondents, who have used their positions to engage in reckless trading, falsify accounting records, breach fiduciary duties, and abuse corporate structures to facilitate a multi-million rand fraud.

The evidence, organized across **6 core legal applications**, demonstrates a deliberate and coordinated effort to defraud creditors and beneficiaries. **CRITICAL new evidence** includes the discovery of a **dual corporate identity** (Villa Via / Kaylovest Three) sharing a single CIPC registration base, and a direct admission by the accountant, Daniel Bantjies, of his intent to **"manufacture"** answers for a SARS query.

We request an immediate investigation, a declaration of delinquency for the directors, and referral for criminal prosecution.

## 4. Key Violations of the Companies Act

### 4.1 Dual Corporate Identity & Reckless Trading (s22, s24, s214)

-   **The Violation:** The suspects maintain two active entities, **Villa Via Arcadia No 2 CC** and **Kaylovest Three (Pty) Ltd**, under the same CIPC registration base (1996/004451). This structure is used to obscure asset ownership, confuse creditors, and facilitate reckless and fraudulent trading.
-   **Evidence:** [RELATION: Dual Corporate Identity](../relations/DUAL_CORPORATE_IDENTITY.md), [EVENT_124](../events/EVENT_124.md)
-   **Violation:** s22 (Reckless Trading), s24 (Corporate Identity), s214 (False Statements).

### 4.2 Falsification of Accounting Records (s28, s214)

-   **The Violation:** On 6 June 2025, when faced with a SARS query, accountant Daniel Bantjies emailed Rynette Farrar stating, **"I will manufacture an answer to the 2 interco invoices over the weekend."** This is a direct admission of intent to falsify accounting records.
-   **Evidence:** [EVENT_119](../events/EVENT_119.md)
-   **Violation:** s28 (Accounting Records), s214 (False Statements).

### 4.3 Gross Breach of Fiduciary Duty & Undisclosed Conflicts (s76, s77)

-   **The Violation:** Bantjies, while acting as accountant for the RegimA entities, was also the CFO of the parent company of a major debtor (Ketoni). He used this conflicted position to manipulate the trust and company books to seize control of an R18.685M payout.
-   **Evidence:** [RELATION: Ketoni Insider Trading Network](../relations/KETONI_INSIDER_TRADING_NETWORK.md)
-   **Violation:** s76 (Director's Duties), s77 (Liability of Directors).

### 4.4 Forgery and Unauthorized Actions (s76)

-   **The Violation:** On 28 June 2024, Rynette Farrar forged the signature "pp Peter" on a trust amendment to unlawfully appoint Bantjies as a trustee, a clear breach of the standards of conduct for anyone involved in a company's affairs.
-   **Evidence:** [RELATION: Trust Capture Sequence](../relations/TRUST_CAPTURE_SEQUENCE.md), [EVENT_103](../events/EVENT_103.md)
-   **Violation:** s76 (Standards of Director's Conduct).

## 5. Relief Sought

2.  **Investigation:** A full investigation into the dual corporate identity of Villa Via / Kaylovest and the associated reckless trading.
