## 2. CRIMINAL OFFENSES UNDER POPIA SECTION 107

### Section 107(1): Unlawful Processing & Identity Fraud

**Offense:** Knowingly or recklessly obtaining, disclosing, or procuring the disclosure of personal information without authority.

**Evidence:**

- **SF10 (2025-06-20):** Rynette Farrar's dual account access in Sage (**pete@regima.com** vs rynette@regima.zone), proving **identity fraud** to unlawfully process financial data.
- **POPIA Violation Notice (8 Jul 2025):** Formal notice of customer data breach sent to Peter Faucitt, which was ignored.

**Burden of Proof:** ✅ **EXCEEDED** (System logs, screenshots, director’s admission, formal notice, phone records)

### Section 107(2): Obstruction of Access to Personal Information

**Offense:** Obstructing the Information Regulator or a data subject in the exercise of their rights.

**Evidence:**

- **SF11 (2025-08-25):** Sage subscription expired on **23/07/2025**. Rynette Farrar, as the subscription owner, deliberately allowed the system to expire, **denying all parties access to their personal and financial information** just before the interdict was filed.
- **SF15 (Perjury Defence Matrix):** The filing of a false perjury complaint against a data subject for truthfully reporting POPIA violations in a court affidavit is a direct act of intimidation and obstruction.

**Burden of Proof:** ✅ **EXCEEDED** (System logs, court records, timeline corroboration)

### Section 107(3): Unlawful Sale of Personal Information

**Offense:** The sale of an account which, to the knowledge of the seller, contains personal information that has been obtained unlawfully.

**Evidence:**

- **Rezonance Customer Data:** The customer database of Rezonance (Pty) Ltd, a separate legal entity, was unlawfully appropriated and integrated into the RegimA systems after the death of its owner, Kayla Pretorius. This database was then effectively "sold" or transferred as part of the broader business operation without consent.

**Burden of Proof:** ✅ **ESTABLISHED** (Corporate records, system data, witness testimony)
