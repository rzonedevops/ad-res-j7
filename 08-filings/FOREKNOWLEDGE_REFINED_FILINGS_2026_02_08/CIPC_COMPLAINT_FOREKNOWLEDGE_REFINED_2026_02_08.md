_## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY_

_This foreknowledge-refined complaint details systematic breaches of the Companies Act and Trust Property Control Act by a syndicate of knowing conspirators, while distinguishing them from parties whose knowledge of the scheme is not proven. The primary conspirators—**Peter Andrew Faucitt, Rynette Farrar, and Daniel Jacobus Bantjies**—are classified as **Tier A Conspirators** based on documentary proof of their premeditated intent (mens rea)._

_Other involved parties, specifically **Kevin Michael Derrick** and **De Novo Business Services / Marisca Meyer**, are classified as **Tier C Participants (Knowledge Unproven)**. While their actions or corporate positions were instrumental to the scheme, there is currently no documentary evidence to prove they had foreknowledge of the unlawful activities. They may be innocent parties who were themselves deceived or whose professional relationships were exploited._

_The complaint establishes an undeniable pattern of fiduciary breach, reckless trading, fraud, and oppression, motivated by the seizure of a **ZAR 18.685 million payout** from **Ketoni Investment Holdings**. The evidence meets and exceeds the standards for civil and criminal liability against the Tier A conspirators, mandating a Section 162 Director Disqualification application and referral for criminal prosecution._
