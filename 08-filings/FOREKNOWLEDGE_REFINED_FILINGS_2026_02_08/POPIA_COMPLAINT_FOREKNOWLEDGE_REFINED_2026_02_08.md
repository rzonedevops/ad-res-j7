_## 2. CRIMINAL OFFENSES UNDER POPIA SECTION 107 (TIER A CONSPIRATORS)_

_The following criminal offenses are alleged against Peter Andrew Faucitt and Rynette Farrar, who acted with proven knowledge and intent._

_### Section 107(1): Unlawful Processing & Identity Fraud_

_**Offense:** Knowingly or recklessly obtaining, disclosing, or procuring the disclosure of personal information without authority._

_**Evidence:**_
_- **SF10 (2025-06-20):** Rynette Farrar's dual account access in Sage (**pete@regima.com** vs rynette@regima.zone), proving **identity fraud** to unlawfully process financial data._
_- **POPIA Violation Notice (8 Jul 2025):** Formal notice of customer data breach sent to Peter Faucitt, which was ignored._

_### Section 107(2): Obstruction of Access to Personal Information_

_**Offense:** Obstructing the Information Regulator or a data subject in the exercise of their rights._

_**Evidence:**_
_- **SF11 (2025-08-25):** Rynette Farrar, as the Sage subscription owner, deliberately allowed the system to expire, **denying all parties access to their personal and financial information**._
_- **SF15 (Perjury Defence Matrix):** The filing of a false perjury complaint against a data subject for truthfully reporting POPIA violations in a court affidavit is a direct act of intimidation and obstruction._

_### Section 107(3): Unlawful Sale of Personal Information_

_**Offense:** The sale of an account which, to the knowledge of the seller, contains personal information that has been obtained unlawfully._

_**Evidence:**_
_- **Rezonance Customer Data:** The customer database of Rezonance (Pty) Ltd, a separate legal entity, was unlawfully appropriated and integrated into the RegimA systems after the death of its owner, Kayla Pretorius._
