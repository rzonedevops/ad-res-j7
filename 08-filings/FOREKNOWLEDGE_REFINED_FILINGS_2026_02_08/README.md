# Foreknowledge Refined Filings - 2026-02-08

## Overview

This directory contains the **foreknowledge-refined** versions of all legal filings for Case 2025-137857. These filings supersede the Super Refined versions by incorporating a rigorous **Foreknowledge Classification Framework** that distinguishes between premeditated conspirators (with documented proof of knowledge) and potentially nescient participants (no evidence of awareness).

## Key Principle

> A person should only be classified as a conspirator where there is **documented, provable evidence** that they had actual knowledge of the unlawful nature of the activities they participated in. Without such evidence, they must be treated as a potentially innocent party who may themselves be a victim of the primary conspirators' deception.

## Classification Tiers

| Tier | Classification | Criteria |
|---|---|---|
| **A** | Premeditated Conspirator | Multiple independent documentary proofs of actual knowledge |
| **B** | Active Participant with Proven Knowledge | At least one direct documentary proof of awareness |
| **C** | Participant — Knowledge Unproven | Participated but no proof they knew activities were unlawful |
| **D** | Structural Connection Only | Connected through corporate structure; no active participation |
