## 3. TAX FRAUD OFFENCES

- Submission of false financial statements

### 3.2 February 2025 Intercompany Pipeline (New Evidence)

**Finding:** A systematic pipeline moved **R2.65M** from Regima SA (RSA) to Regima Skin Treatments (RST) in Feb 2025. RSA collected revenue from customers and transferred the bulk funds to RST using vague descriptions like "Internet Trf To Transfer".

**Evidence:**
- **FIN-FEB25-001:** RSA bank statement showing 37 outflows totaling R2.65M.
- **FIN-FEB25-002:** RST bank statement showing 9 inflows from RSA totaling R2.96M.
- **SARS-FEB25-001:** Email from Rynette to Bantjies (5 Jun 2025) referencing a "SARS query re the two big inter company invoices at the end of February 2025."
- **SARS-FEB25-003:** Email from Rynette to Bantjies (6 Jun 2025) stating: "**The two big invoices were done on your request**, for which I have no answer."

**Implication:** This provides direct evidence of a scheme to obscure revenue and potentially evade tax. The invoices were not for genuine commercial transactions but were created post-facto at the accountant's request to justify cash movements that were already under SARS scrutiny.

### 3.3 Historical Pattern of Abuse

The Feb 2025 pipeline is not an isolated incident. Analysis of historical data shows a cumulative transfer of over **R25 million** from RSA to RST since 2021, indicating a long-standing practice of revenue stream hijacking.

### 3.4 Willful Intent and Concealment

The email correspondence between Rynette and Bantjies demonstrates willful intent. They are aware of the SARS query and are actively discussing how to formulate a response for invoices that Rynette admits she has "no answer" for. This is evidence of a conspiracy to mislead the revenue service.

Furthermore, Rynette's instructions to the bookkeepers at De Novo to create "loan accounts" for other entities to absorb expenses not belonging to Regima SA is further proof of a deliberate strategy to misrepresent the financial position of the companies.

**Evidence:**
- **SARS-FEB25-009:** Rynette instructs De Novo to create a "loan account within RegimA SA, for the other company."
- **SARS-FEB25-010:** Rynette instructs De Novo to post expenses for "Coral draw and Quickbooks" to "Rezonance's loan account."
