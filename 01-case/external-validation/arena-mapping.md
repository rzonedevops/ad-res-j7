## SARS Tax Compliance Validation

### Legal Framework

**Statute**: Tax Administration Act 28 of 2011

**Principles**:
- `bona-fides` (good faith tax reporting)
- `lex/adm/` - Administrative compliance standards
- Revenue reporting obligations
- Expense verification requirements

**Validation Standards**:
- Compliance certificates
- Tax returns filed and accepted
- No outstanding debt
- No audits or investigations

**Strength**: ⭐⭐⭐ STRONG - Government confirmation

## Banking Good Standing

### Legal Framework

**Principles**:
- `bona-fides` (good banking relationships)
- Financial conduct standards
- `lex/cmp/` - Corporate banking requirements

**Validation Standards**:
- Account status (active, good standing)
- No fraud flags or investigations
- Positive business relationship history
- Banking conduct assessment

**Strength**: ⭐⭐ MEDIUM - Bank confirmation

## Forensic Accountant Review

### Legal Framework

**Statute**: Accounting Profession Act 26 of 2005 (professional standards)

**Principles**:
- `lex/prof-eth/` - Professional ethics
- Expert opinion standards
- Industry benchmarking
- Reasonableness assessments

**Validation Standards**:
- Independent financial statement review
- Director loan account analysis
- IT spend reasonableness vs industry norms
- Overall financial management quality

**Strength**: ⭐⭐⭐ STRONG - Expert professional opinion
