## 3. THE ABSA ACCOUNT STRUCTURE

### 3.1 Eight Unauthorized Accounts

3.1.1 Eight ABSA bank accounts were opened. These accounts were established outside the FNB banking framework where the SOLE mandate operated — a framework that gave the Second Respondent independent authority.

3.1.2 The strategic purpose of the ABSA migration:

| FNB Framework | ABSA Framework |
|---------------|----------------|
| SOLE mandate — Daniel has independent authority | New accounts — Daniel has no access |
| FNB Legal confirmed Daniel's authority (18 Jun 2025) | No mandate confirmation obtained |
| Established banking relationship | New relationship, new signatories |

3.1.3 The migration to ABSA achieved through identity fraud what the Applicant could not achieve through legitimate corporate governance: the exclusion of the Second Respondent from banking operations.

### 3.2 Use of Stolen Identity Details

3.2.1 The ABSA accounts were opened using the Second Respondent's stolen card details — the same card details associated with the cards secretly cancelled on 7 June 2025.

3.2.2 This constitutes identity fraud under common law and under FICA s29 (which prohibits the making of false statements in connection with financial transactions).
