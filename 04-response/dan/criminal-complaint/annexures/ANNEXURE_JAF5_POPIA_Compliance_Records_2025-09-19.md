### POPIA Section 109 - Administrative Fines

**Provision:**
> "The Regulator may impose an administrative fine not exceeding R10 000 000 or 2% of annual turnover, whichever is the greater, on a responsible party who fails to comply with the provisions of this Act."

---

### Potential Non-Compliance Issues

**1. Data Subject Access Request Delay (Section 23)**

**Non-Compliance:** Failure to respond within 30-day statutory deadline

**Likelihood:** Medium (extension granted, but delay caused by obstruction)

**Fine Range:** R100,000 - R500,000

**Reasoning:**
- Data subject right of access is fundamental (Section 23)
- Delay caused by obstruction, not systemic failure
- Extension granted and request eventually fulfilled
- Mitigating factors: Good faith, external cause

**Non-Compliance:** Records not updated for 4-day period (September 19-23, 2025)

**Reasoning:**
- Section 51 requires up-to-date records
- 4-day delay relatively short
- Records updated immediately after obstruction resolved
- Mitigating factors: Short duration, prompt remediation
