## Part 2: POPIA Legal Framework

### 2.1 Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA)

**Act Number:** Act 4 of 2013  
**Commencement Date:** July 1, 2020 (full implementation)  
**Regulatory Authority:** Information Regulator (South Africa)

**Purpose:** Regulate the processing of personal information to protect individuals' privacy while balancing legitimate interests of entities that process personal information.

---

### 2.2 Information Officer Requirements

**Section 55 - Appointment of Information Officer:**

> "Every responsible party must appoint an information officer who shall be responsible for ensuring compliance with the conditions for the lawful processing of personal information."

**Information Officer Duties (Section 55):**

1. **Compliance Oversight:** Ensure responsible party complies with POPIA
2. **Internal Measures:** Develop and implement internal measures for compliance
3. **Training:** Ensure staff are trained on POPIA requirements
4. **Request Handling:** Handle requests for access to personal information
5. **Complaint Management:** Manage complaints and queries from data subjects
6. **Regulator Liaison:** Liaise with Information Regulator
7. **Record Keeping:** Maintain records of processing activities
8. **Breach Notification:** Report data breaches to Regulator and data subjects

---

### 2.3 Registration Requirements

**Section 56 - Registration of Information Officers:**

> "Every information officer must be registered with the Regulator within a period determined by the Regulator."

**Registration Process:**
1. Submit registration application to Information Regulator
2. Provide organisation details and information officer details
3. Pay registration fee
4. Receive registration certificate
5. Renew registration as required

**Jacqueline's Registration:** Registered November 1, 2025 (Regulator Registration Number: 2025-065686)
