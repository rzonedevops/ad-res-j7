#### 5.2 Legal Framework

**Companies Act 2008 Requirements:**

**Section 45 (Financial Assistance):**
- Applies to loans TO directors
- Does not apply to director loan accounts (directors lending TO company)
- Board resolution required for loans TO directors
- Not required for director withdrawals from existing loan accounts

**RegimA Context:**
- Director loan accounts show directors lending TO companies
- Directors withdraw from their own loan accounts (repayment of loans)
- Section 45 does not apply
- Board resolutions not required

**Legal Opinion:** Director loan account withdrawals (repayments of director loans to company) do not require board resolutions under Companies Act 2008.
