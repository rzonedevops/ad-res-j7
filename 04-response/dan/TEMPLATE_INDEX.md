## Template Structure Overview

All responses follow this 6-part structure:

1. **Introduction** - Set context (1-2 paragraphs)
2. **Factual Corrections** - Fix inaccuracies (2-3 minimum)
3. **Counter-Evidence** - Provide documentary proof (3-5 pieces)
4. **Material Non-Disclosures** - Expose omissions (1-3 minimum)
5. **Demonstrate Inconsistency** - Show contradictions (1+ minimum)
6. **Strategic Counter-Narrative** - Present full picture
7. **Causation Analysis** - If applicable (highly recommended when Peter caused the problem)
8. **Supporting Evidence** - List all annexures with descriptions
9. **Cross-References** - Link to comprehensive documents

## Quality Standards

### Minimum Requirements
- ✅ All template sections completed
- ✅ 2-3 factual corrections with evidence
- ✅ 3-5 pieces of counter-evidence
- ✅ 1-3 material non-disclosures
- ✅ 1+ inconsistency demonstrated
- ✅ Comprehensive counter-narrative
- ✅ All annexures listed with descriptions
- ✅ Cross-references included

### Optimal Range
- 📝 Word count: 1,000-1,500 words
- 📄 Annexures: 5-10 specific references
- 🔗 Cross-references: 3-5 comprehensive documents
- 🎯 Professional tone throughout
- ⚡ Strategic impact: Undermines Peter's claims
