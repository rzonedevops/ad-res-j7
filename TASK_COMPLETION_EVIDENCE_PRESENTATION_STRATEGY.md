## KEY INNOVATION: CENTRAL REVELATION FRAMEWORK

### The Core Discovery

**Dan & Kay's Shopify platform (owned by RegimA Zone Ltd UK) was paid for by this UK company while RWD ZA generated R2.94M-R6.88M revenue without compensation and has no independent revenue stream.**

### Five Core Facts (Every Evidence Piece Links Back)

1. **Platform Ownership:** RegimA Zone Ltd (UK company owned by Dan & Kay) owns the Shopify platform
   - Evidence: JF-DAN-SYSTEM5

2. **Platform Costs:** RegimA Zone Ltd paid R140,000 - R280,000 over 28 months
   - Evidence: JF-DAN-SYSTEM6

3. **Revenue Generation:** RWD ZA generated R2.94M - R6.88M on this platform
   - Evidence: JF-DAN-SYSTEM7

4. **No Compensation:** RWD ZA paid R 0.00 to platform owner
   - Evidence: JF-BS1 (Bank statements)

5. **Complete Dependency:** RWD ZA has no independent revenue stream (100% dependent on Dan & Kay's platform)
   - Evidence: JF-DAN-SYSTEM1-4

### Strategic Impact

This framework transforms the case from **defensive** (responding to Peter's allegations) to **offensive** (exposing systematic unjust enrichment by RWD ZA).

**Comparative Framing:**
- Peter questions: R500,000 payment
- Peter ignores: R2,940,000 - R6,880,000 unjust enrichment
- **Ratio:** 1:6 to 1:14 (unjust enrichment is 6-14 times larger than questioned amount)

### R500K Payment → Unjust Enrichment Context

**Peter's Allegation:** "Unauthorized R500,000 payment with no business purpose"

**Strategy Response:**
"The Applicant questions R500,000 while remaining silent about R2.94M-R6.88M in unjust enrichment by his own company. RWD ZA used a platform owned and paid for by RegimA Zone Ltd to generate millions in revenue. Not one rand was paid to the platform owner. The questioned R500,000 represents only 7-17% of the unjust enrichment the Applicant ignores."

### System Access → Property Rights

**Peter's Allegation:** "Respondents interfering through unauthorized system access"

**Strategy Response:**
"The Applicant seeks to restrict the Second Respondent from accessing a platform that the Second Respondent owns through RegimA Zone Ltd. RegimA Zone Ltd paid R140K-R280K for this platform. RWD ZA used it to generate R2.94M-R6.88M without compensating the owner. The Applicant cannot exclude property owners from their own property."
