## 1. Scope of Mandate (Unbundled Legal Services)

1.1. The Instructing Parties hereby appoint the Correspondent to act as their attorney of record in the High Court of South Africa (Gauteng Division) under Case Number **2025-137857** (the "Proceedings").

1.2. This is a **limited mandate** for unbundled legal services. The Correspondent is instructed **strictly** to perform the following functions:
a) Provide a physical address for service within 25km of the courthouse;
b) Receive service of documents from the opposing party and forward them immediately to the Instructing Parties;
c) File documents on Court Online as prepared and provided by the Instructing Parties;
d) Appear at court hearings to present the documents and arguments as instructed by the Instructing Parties; and
e) Attend to procedural matters (e.g., drawing files, enrolling matters) as instructed.

1.3. The Correspondent is **NOT** instructed to:
a) Draft affidavits, notices, or pleadings;
b) Conduct legal research or formulate legal strategy; or
c) Negotiate settlements without express written instruction.

2.1. The Instructing Parties acknowledge that the Correspondent is acting for multiple corporate entities in the same Proceedings.
2.2. The Instructing Parties confirm that their interests in the Proceedings are aligned (defending against the applicant's interdict/contempt applications) and that no conflict of interest currently exists between them.
2.3. The Instructing Parties hereby waive any potential conflict of interest arising from this joint representation.
