- **The Lie:** Mr. Faucitt swore under oath (Founding Affidavit, MAT4719 pages 10–38, sworn at Bedfordview SAPS on 13 August 2025) that his co-directors were making "unauthorized" transactions.

The concept of an "unauthorized" transaction by a co-director is a legal and factual impossibility.

### 1.2. Provable Foreknowledge and Perjury

- By swearing in his affidavit that transactions required his authority, Mr. Faucitt committed **perjury with provable knowledge**. He knew his central claim was false.
- **MAT4719 Annexure PF11** (page 162) further proves Peter's awareness: FNB sent him an SMS stating **"80% of international limit reached"** — he was actively monitoring the spending he later characterised as "unauthorised."
