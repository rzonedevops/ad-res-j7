## 3. NEW EVIDENCE OF CRIMINAL VIOLATIONS (v6)

### 3.1. Section 107(2): Unlawful Processing & Identity Fraud via Email Impersonation

Stylometric analysis of over 6,000 emails proves that Rynette Farrar unlawfully processed the personal information of Peter Faucitt by operating his email account (`pete@regima.com`) for years. This constitutes a severe, long-term security compromise and a violation of the conditions for lawful processing.

*   **The Violation:** By impersonating a director, Rynette Farrar gained unauthorized access to a trove of personal and special personal information, which she then used to commit further crimes, including fraud and theft.
*   **Significance:** This is a foundational breach of POPIA. The responsible party (RegimA Worldwide) not only failed to secure personal information but was, in fact, the instrument of its abuse.
*   **Evidence:** `[EVENT_113: Stylometric Analysis Reveals Identity Impersonation](../events/EVENT_113.md)`

### 3.2. Section 107(2): Unlawful Processing in CIPC Erasure Campaign

Beginning in November 2023, Rynette Farrar unlawfully processed the personal information of the deceased, Kayla Pretorius, in a campaign to have her removed from CIPC records. This involved providing false information to the company's CIPC agent.

*   **The Violation:** This is a direct contravention of Section 107(2). The personal information of a data subject was unlawfully processed to alter a public record for fraudulent ends.
*   **Significance:** This act demonstrates a callous disregard for the law and the dignity of the deceased, all in service of a criminal conspiracy to erase a key figure from the company's history.
*   **Evidence:** `[EVENT_112: CIPC Erasure Campaign](../events/EVENT_112.md)`

### 3.3. Section 107(1): Obstruction of the Regulator via SARS eFiling Impersonation

On 26 April 2024, Rynette Farrar impersonated Danie Bantjies on the SARS eFiling system. By using his credentials to unlawfully alter official records, she obstructed the lawful functions of both SARS and, by extension, the Information Regulator, as this action was a precursor to large-scale financial fraud that impacted the personal information of countless data subjects (employees, customers, and suppliers).

*   **The Violation:** This act of identity fraud on a government platform constitutes a severe obstruction of a public body in the performance of its duties.
*   **Evidence:** `[EVENT_111: SARS eFiling Impersonation](../events/EVENT_111.md)`
