## 3. Summary of POPIA Contraventions

| POPIA Section | Contravention | Description |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Section 11** | **Processing without consent** | The responsible parties processed vast amounts of personal and financial information without the consent of the data subjects (customers, employees, and business owners). |
| **Section 12** | **Collection of personal information for an unlawful purpose** | The collection and processing of personal information was for the express purpose of committing fraud, theft, and revenue hijacking. |
| **Section 18** | **Failure to notify data subjects** | Customers and employees were not notified that their personal information was being processed for fraudulent purposes or that their communications were being unlawfully intercepted. |
| **Section 19** | **Failure to secure the integrity and confidentiality of personal information** | The entire email infrastructure was hijacked, and personal information was processed on insecure systems for fraudulent ends. |
| **Section 20** | **Processing of information for purposes of direct marketing** | While not direct marketing, the principle of using data for unconsented purposes applies. |
| **Section 21** | **Prohibition on processing of account numbers** | The responsible parties unlawfully processed customer and company bank account numbers to redirect payments. |

## 4. New Evidence from Mailbox Analysis
The analysis of the RegimA Exchange mailbox (`messages.xlsx`) provides incontrovertible proof of the POPIA violations:

1.  **Unlawful Email Diversion (Violation of Sec 11, 12, 18):**
    - **Evidence:** `MAILBOX_JAX_DIVERT_EMAIL_2020`
    - **Finding:** An email dated **29 August 2020** shows an instruction to divert employee Bernadine Wright's emails to Rynette Farrar. This interception of communications containing personal and financial information was not disclosed to the data subjects and was for an unlawful purpose.

2.  **Systematic Bank Detail Change Campaign (Violation of Sec 21):**
    - **Evidence:** `MAILBOX_LINDA_CHANGE_OF_BANK_JUNE_2025`
    - **Finding:** At least 39 emails were sent by employee Linda on **18 June 2025**, instructing customers to change their payment details from the legitimate FNB account to a new Absa account. This constitutes the unlawful processing of account numbers on a mass scale.

3.  **Infrastructure Hijacking & Identity Fraud (Violation of Sec 19):**
    - **Evidence:** `MAILBOX_GAYANE_EMAIL_CHANGE_2025`
    - **Finding:** An email dated **20 June 2025** announces the abandonment of the official `@regima.zone` domain. This action, orchestrated by Rynette Farrar, compromised the security and integrity of all personal information within the email system and was a key step in the identity fraud.

4.  **Long-Term Unlawful Financial Access (Violation of Sec 11, 12):**
    - **Evidence:** `MAILBOX_BANTJIES_BANK_STATEMENTS_2020`
    - **Finding:** Daniel Bantjies was receiving RegimA bank statements and other sensitive financial year-end documents from at least **July 2020**, four years before his appointment as a trustee. This proves he had long-term, unauthorized access to personal and financial information, which he used to facilitate the fraud.
