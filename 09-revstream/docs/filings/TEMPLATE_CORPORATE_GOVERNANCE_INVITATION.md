do hereby make oath and state that:

1. I am an adult female businesswoman. I depose to this affidavit in my capacity as the founder, sole member of the Second Respondent (RegimA Skin Treatments CC), and sole director of the Third Respondent (RegimA Zone (Pty) Ltd).
2. The facts contained herein fall within my personal knowledge, unless the context indicates otherwise, and are both true and correct.
3. I depose to this affidavit strictly in my representative capacity on behalf of the corporate entities (the Second and Third Respondents), and not in my personal capacity as the First Respondent.
4. This affidavit is filed not to oppose any specific application, nor to breach any existing court order, but rather to fulfill my fiduciary duties to the corporate entities under the Companies Act 71 of 2008 and the Close Corporations Act 69 of 1984.

**PURPOSE OF THIS AFFIDAVIT**

5. The corporate entities note the ongoing litigation initiated by the Applicant. The entities recognize that the Applicant has expressed deep dissatisfaction with the current management, operations, and strategic direction of the businesses.
6. As the sole director/member, I have a fiduciary duty to act in the best interests of the companies. This includes considering all feedback that could improve the entities' operations, profitability, and governance.
7. The entities cannot operate optimally while mired in destructive litigation. Therefore, the Second and Third Respondents hereby extend a formal, good-faith invitation to the Applicant to engage constructively regarding the future of the businesses.

**FORMAL INVITATION FOR FEEDBACK**

8. The Second and Third Respondents formally invite the Applicant to provide written, constructive feedback on the following operational areas:
    8.1. **Strategic Direction:** What specific changes to the business model or product lines does the Applicant propose?
    8.2. **Financial Management:** What specific financial controls or reporting mechanisms does the Applicant believe are currently lacking?
    8.3. **Corporate Governance:** What specific governance structures does the Applicant suggest implementing to ensure transparency and accountability?
    8.4. **Operational Efficiency:** What specific operational inefficiencies has the Applicant identified, and what are his proposed solutions?
9. The entities request that the Applicant provide this feedback in a structured, professional format, rather than through the mechanism of urgent court applications.
10. The entities undertake to review any such constructive feedback in good faith, at the next formal management meeting, and to consider implementing any suggestions that are demonstrably in the best interests of the companies.

**CONCLUSION**

11. This invitation is extended in the spirit of corporate preservation. The entities' primary objective is to continue trading, serving clients, and employing staff without the disruption of ongoing legal hostilities.
12. We await the Applicant's constructive proposals via his attorneys of record.
