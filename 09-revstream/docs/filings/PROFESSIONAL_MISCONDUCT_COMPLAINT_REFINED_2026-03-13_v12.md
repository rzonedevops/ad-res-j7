## 3. Executive Summary of Misconduct

This complaint details gross professional misconduct by registered accountants Daniel Jacobus Bantjies (SAICA) and Marisca Meyer (SAIPA), who have abused their professional status to act as key enablers in a criminal enterprise. Their actions, which violate the fundamental principles of the accounting profession, have facilitated fraud, tax evasion, perjury, and breaches of trust with a total quantum exceeding R63M.

The evidence is conclusive and includes a direct admission by Bantjies of his intent to **"manufacture"** answers for SARS. We request an immediate investigation and the institution of disciplinary proceedings to revoke their professional designations.

## 4. Grounds for Complaint & Violation of Professional Codes

### 4.1 Admission of Intent to Defraud SARS (Bantjies)

-   **The Misconduct:** On 6 June 2025, Bantjies admitted in an email his intent to "manufacture an answer" for a SARS query about fraudulent invoices. This is a direct violation of the principles of integrity and professional behavior.
-   **Evidence:** [EVENT_119](../events/EVENT_119.md)
-   **Violation:** SAICA Code of Professional Conduct (s110, s111, s115).

### 4.2 Gross Breach of Fiduciary Duty & Conflict of Interest (Bantjies)

-   **The Misconduct:** Bantjies operated under at least six irreconcilable conflicts of interest, most notably acting as CFO for a major debtor (Ketoni) while also being the complicit trustee for the creditor (FFT). He used this position to commit perjury and seize control of an ZAR 18.685M payout.
-   **Evidence:** [RELATION: Ketoni Insider Trading Network](../relations/KETONI_INSIDER_TRADING_NETWORK.md)
-   **Violation:** SAICA Code (s112 - Objectivity, s113 - Professional Competence and Due Care), Trust Property Control Act.

### 4.3 Complicity in Forgery and Fraud (Bantjies)

-   **The Misconduct:** Bantjies knowingly accepted a trustee position that was granted to him via a forged document and then proceeded to swear a perjured affidavit to support a fraudulent court application.
-   **Evidence:** [RELATION: Trust Capture Sequence](../relations/TRUST_CAPTURE_SEQUENCE.md)
-   **Violation:** SAICA Code (s111 - Integrity).

### 4.4 Signing Fraudulent Financial Statements (Meyer)

-   **The Misconduct:** Marisca Meyer of De Novo Business Services knowingly prepared and signed off on fraudulent, backdated financial statements at the instruction of a non-director (Rynette Farrar), lending an air of legitimacy to the criminal enterprise.
-   **Evidence:** Financial Statements, communication records.
-   **Violation:** SAIPA Code of Conduct (Fundamental Principles).

## 5. Request for Disciplinary Action

The conduct of Bantjies and Meyer represents a grave threat to the integrity of the accounting profession in South Africa. Their actions are not mere negligence; they are willful and criminal. We urge SAICA and SAIPA to act decisively to protect the public by revoking their professional designations and referring them for criminal prosecution.
