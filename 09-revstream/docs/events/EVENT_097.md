## Description

On 4 November 2025, due to further egregious conduct by Jacqueline Faucitt, Peter Faucitt was constrained to lodge a second urgent application against her under Case No. 2025-137857. This application sought to have the order of 19 August 2025 varied rather than pursue contempt proceedings at that time.

The application was opposed and was ultimately dismissed. The presiding Judge stated that Peter had an alternate recourse, namely contempt of court proceedings. This judicial direction is the basis for the subsequent contempt application filed in February 2026.

The Judge's statement constitutes an implicit acknowledgment that the court order was being breached.
