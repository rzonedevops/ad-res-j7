## Description

On 28 June 2024, Rynette Farrar forged Peter Faucitt's signature — signing "pp Peter" — on the trust amendment resolution to install Danie Bantjies as a third trustee of the Faucitt Family Trust. This is the **documentary proof date** from the Trust Amendment Document and SF15.

The email sent by Rynette to Bantjies on 28 June 2024 reads:

> "Hi Danie  
> I have just signed the resolution to have you installed as a trustee on the Faucitt Family Trust, however, I need a copy of your ID to finalise this process if you could be so kind.  
> Regards  
> pp Peter"

Bantjies responded on 1 July 2024 with his ID document, completing the process.

## Critical Significance

This event is significant for three reasons:

1. **Forgery:** Rynette had no authority to sign "pp Peter" on a trust amendment. A trust resolution to appoint a new trustee requires the signature of the existing trustees (Peter and Jacqui). Rynette was not a trustee.
2. **10-Day Proximity to Sage Affidavit:** The forgery on 28 June 2024 places it exactly **10 days** before the Sage "Invited User Switch" affidavit on 8-9 July 2024 (EVENT_063/Phase 2), suggesting coordinated action rather than coincidence.
3. **Bantjies Installation as Conspiracy Enabler:** Installing a conflicted accountant as trustee — one who owed R18.685M to the trust via Ketoni — created a compliant third trustee who would later certify Peter's perjured affidavit (EVENT_049, 13 August 2025) and swear his own confirmatory affidavit for the ex parte interdict.

## Distinction from EVENT_059 (11 August 2025)

This event (EVENT_103) documents the **underlying forgery** on the trust amendment document dated 28 June 2024. It must be distinguished from:

- **EVENT_104** (11 August 2025): Rynette emails Bantjies the "Main Trustee" appointment communication, backdated to 1 July 2025.
- **EVENT_059** (11 August 2025): The broader settlement/interdict coordination.

The 28 June 2024 date is the **forgery date**. The 11 August 2025 date is the **backdated appointment communication date**.
