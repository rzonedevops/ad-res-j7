## Description

On 28 August 2025, Jacqui Faucitt forwarded the "Important Update: Change of Email Address" email (originally sent by Gayane Williams on 20 June 2025) to S Munga at ENS Africa (smunga@ensafrica.com) under the subject line **"E-MAILS CANCELLED"**.

The email was sent from jfaucitt@proton.me, indicating Jacqui was using Proton Mail rather than the regima.zone Exchange system. The forwarded content contained the full original email instructing contacts to migrate from @regima.zone to @regimaskin.co.za.

## Forensic Significance

This event establishes that Jacqui Faucitt was aware of the email domain migration and communicated it to the ENS Africa legal team. The timing is significant: the ex parte interdict was granted on **19 August 2025** — just 9 days before this email. ENS Africa was the legal firm involved in the mediation process (ORG_022). The fact that Jacqui described the migration as "E-MAILS CANCELLED" rather than a routine change suggests she understood the disruptive nature of the action.
