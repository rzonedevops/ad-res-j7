## Description

On 26 August 2025, a payment of **ZAR 300,000** was made from the Villa Via Arcadia No 2 CC bank account (62423540807) to S Munga at ENS Africa attorneys. This payment was made from a company entity account to fund legal proceedings that were purportedly personal in nature.

## Evidence Source

**Document:** `2026-02-17-SupportingEvidenceRequestedbyJacquiReAccountBalances&Activity.pdf` — Email from Daniel Faucitt to Jacqui Faucitt (CC: Rudi Pottas, Stef, Bobo Mshengu), dated 17 February 2026, referencing the FNB account statements showing this payment.

## Forensic Significance

This payment establishes a critical precedent: the company entities were used to fund legal costs related to the interdict proceedings. When Peter Faucitt later denied that entity funds should cover legal costs for the respondents, this payment proves that the entities had already been used to pay the applicant's own legal costs. The payment was made just **7 days after** the ex parte interdict was granted (19 August 2025), and **2 days before** Jacqui forwarded the "E-MAILS CANCELLED" email to the same ENS Africa attorney.
