# Rezonance Debt Manipulation (EVENT_017)

## Event Metadata

**Date:** 2023-02-28 to 2025-02-28

**Description:** Financial records from Sage Business Cloud Accounting reveal a systematic manipulation of the debt owed by RegimA Skin Treatments CC (RST) to Rezonance (Pty) Ltd (Kayla Pretorius's company). In February 2023, RST owed Rezonance R1,035,081.90. Following Kayla's death in July 2023, the debt was systematically paid down in R50,000 increments until it reached R0.00 in February 2024. However, by February 2025, the balance had reversed, with Rezonance now purportedly owing RST R223,878.93. This reversal was achieved by fraudulently misallocating GoDaddy domain and hosting payments to the Rezonance account, effectively making the R1M+ debt "disappear" and turning the creditor into a debtor.
