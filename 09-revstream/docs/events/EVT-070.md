## Description

On 20 June 2025, Gayane Williams sent an email titled **"Important Update: Change of Email Address"** from gayane@regima.zone to gayane@regimaskin.co.za (with CC to kent@regimaskin.co.za, orders@regimaskin.co.za, admin@regimaskin.co.za, el@regimaskin.co.za), instructing all contacts to stop using @regima.zone email addresses and redirect all correspondence to @regimaskin.co.za addresses.

The email was sent in multiple batches between 12:49 and 13:11 UTC, reaching at least 18 mailboxes across the regima.zone Exchange tenant. The email was sent both via Exchange (to internal users) and via Proton Mail (to external regimaskin.co.za addresses).
