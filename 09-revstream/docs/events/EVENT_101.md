## Legal Significance

This contempt application represents a critical escalation in the proceedings. The Judge in the dismissed variation application (4 November 2025) explicitly directed Peter toward contempt proceedings as the appropriate remedy. The application demonstrates a pattern of wilful non-compliance with the court order, including:

1. Frustrating business operations by blocking stock removal
2. Failing to surrender bank cards as ordered
3. Removing employee's work phone
4. Intimidating staff with a body guard
5. Giving notice of withdrawal from settlement agreements signed under duress (22 Sep 2025)
6. Refusing to allow stock takes

The application seeks imprisonment as a sanction, reflecting the severity and persistence of the contempt.
