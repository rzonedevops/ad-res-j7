## Key Admissions

### 1. Suspected Fraud Acknowledgment
> "Following my recent written request to cancel the 3 x debit cards on the above account due to suspected fraud..."

**Legal Significance:** Director explicitly acknowledges awareness of fraudulent activity on the company account.

### 2. Prior Card Cancellation Request
> "...my recent written request to cancel the 3 x debit cards..."

**Legal Significance:** Confirms premeditated action to restrict account access, corroborating EVENT_012 (Secret Card Cancellations).

### 3. Accessory After the Fact Warning
> "This action may place FNB in the untenable position of being an accessory after the fact, should such fraud be proven..."

**Legal Significance:** Peter warns FNB about criminal liability - the same standard applies to Peter himself as director with knowledge of fraud.

### 4. Exchange Control Violations
> "I also believe that due to the amount of dollar-based subscription expense passing through this account our company may be transgressing the Exchange Control Regulations..."

**Legal Significance:** Director's admission provides basis for SARB investigation into international transaction violations.
