# Phase 1: The Invitation (18 May 2020)

**Event:** Rynette Farrar is invited as a standard user to the RegimA Worldwide Sage Accounting system.

**Date:** 18 May 2020

**Significance:** This document establishes the baseline for Rynette Farrar's access to the Sage system. She was not the original owner or administrator; she was an **invited user**. The invitation was sent by **Kayla Pretorius**, who was the legitimate administrator of the account at the time.

---

## The Invitation Email

The following email was sent to Rynette Farrar, providing her initial access to the system. This is the foundational piece of evidence proving her user status.

| Attribute | Value |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Date** | `2020-05-18 06:40:02+00:00` |
| **From** | `noreply@accounting.sageone.co.za` |
| **To** | `rynette@regima.zone` |
| **Subject** | `Accounting: You have been Invited` |

### Email Body

> "Hi Rynette
> 
> **Kayla Pretorius has invited you to access RegimA Worldwide Distribution (Pty) Ltd on Accounting.** By accepting this invitation, you will have access to the company information and be able to process transactions online, anywhere, anytime.
> 
> To accept this invitation and activate your user account, please click the following link..."

---

## Analysis

This email is irrefutable proof of the following:

1.  **Kayla Pretorius was the Administrator:** The invitation was initiated by her, demonstrating her ownership and control over the Sage company file.
2.  **Rynette Farrar was a subordinate User:** She was granted access by the administrator, not the other way around.
3.  **The Company File is "RegimA Worldwide Distribution (Pty) Ltd":** This confirms the specific entity to which she was granted access.

This event is the starting point of the timeline. All subsequent actions must be viewed in the context of Rynette's initial, limited user permissions.
