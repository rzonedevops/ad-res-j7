# Phase 2: The "Invited User Switch" (July 2024)

**Event:** The fraudulent transfer of the Sage Worldwide account ownership from the deceased Kayla Pretorius to Peter Faucitt, orchestrated by Rynette Farrar.

**Date:** 08 July 2024 - 09 July 2024

**Significance:** This is the central act of the Sage account takeover. It demonstrates a clear intent to deceive Sage support and seize control of the company’s primary financial system. The email thread provides a direct, undeniable audit trail of the fraud.

## Analysis

This exchange is the smoking gun of the Sage takeover. Key points:

1.  **Fraudulent Affidavit:** The form required the signature of the current admin, Kayla Pretorius, who had been deceased for a year. The affidavit submitted by Rynette was therefore, by definition, fraudulent.
2.  **Intent to Deceive:** Rynette knew Kayla was deceased and could not authorize the transfer. By submitting the form, she actively deceived Sage into performing an unauthorized action.
3.  **Peter Faucitt as Beneficiary:** The ownership was transferred directly to Peter Faucitt, a co-conspirator, solidifying their joint control over the company's finances.
4.  **False Transparency:** CC'ing Jacqui Faucitt on the email was a calculated move to create a veneer of legitimacy, making it appear as though the switch was an open, agreed-upon administrative change.

This event marks the successful seizure of the Sage Worldwide account, a critical asset for controlling the company's revenue and financial records.
