ACCOUNTING AND TAX SERVICES

Thank you for the opportunity to present our proposal for reconstructing the accounting records and
assisting with SARS submissions.

Our modus operandi is to complement existing management and ensure accurate reporting. We
ensure that good financial management is applied. We also make sure that system and controls are
implemented and maintained.

Our services RegimA Sa will comprise the following:
e Obtaining access to e-filing for the entity
e Write-up of all bank accounts for the entity, possibly from May 2021 to current
e Possibly compiling annual financial statements from 2022 to 2025
e Submitting income tax returns for these periods

We are dependent on obtaining the last submission to SARS to determine the exact date from which
we must start the project. We will also try and get CSV format bank statement as far as possible to
reduce the amount of time spend on write-ups.

Depending on what was submitted to SARS as well as the outcome of the write-ups there may be a
requirement to draft Annual Financial Statement.

For both the e-filing access process, which may include the appointment of a public officer with SARS,
and the write-ups, we proposed an hourly charge. For this process we will charge R425 per hour of
work (four hundred and twenty-five rand) (excluding Vat) these will be levied on 25th of each month and
considering that these are salaried resources, payment will be within 7 days of invoice.

Should we be required to draft annual financial statements we propose that we stick to a compilation
engagement. This should be sufficient given the size of the entity. Here we propose a fee of R7 000
(seven thousand rand) (excluding VAT) per year to be drafted.

The submission of income tax returns attracts a fee if R1 250 (one thousand two hundred and fifty rand)
(excluding VAT) per return.

De Novo reserves the right to negotiate.
