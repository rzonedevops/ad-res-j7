# Rynette Organogram Analysis - 2025-04-12

## Document Title
"How Sales Work" - Sales Process Flow Diagram

## Key Entities Identified

### Persons
- **Kent** - Sales/Order processing role (receives orders via phone/email, receives POP)
- **EL** - Sales/Order processing role (receives orders via phone/email, receives POP)

### Systems/Platforms
- **Shopify** - E-commerce platform used for:
  - Creating Proforma Invoices
  - Order fulfillment marking
  - Automatic order pull-through to accounting
  
- **SAGE** - Accounting system used for:
  - Tax Invoice creation (automatic)
  - Automatic email of Tax Invoices to customers

- **Courier Guy** - Logistics/delivery service:
  - Shipment loading on portal
  - Tracking number generation
  - Parcel collection
  - Delivery/notification of problems
  - Success/return notification

## Sales Process Flow

### Step 1: Order Receipt
- Customer contacts via Phone or Email
- Orders received by Kent or EL

### Step 2: Proforma Invoice
- Kent or EL creates Proforma Invoice in Shopify
- Proforma Invoice emailed to customer

### Step 3: Payment
- Customer pays Proforma Invoice
- Customer emails Proof of Payment (POP) to Kent or EL

### Step 4: Order Fulfillment
- Kent or EL receives POP
- Order marked as fulfilled in Shopify

### Step 5: Tax Invoice Generation
- Once order marked fulfilled in Shopify
- Order automatically pulled through to SAGE
- Tax Invoice created automatically in SAGE
- Tax Invoice automatically emailed to customer

### Step 6: Shipping
- Shipment loaded on Courier Guy portal
- Tracking number generated
- Courier Guy collects parcels
- Delivery or problem notification
- Final delivery success/return notification

## Evidence Significance

### For Revenue Stream Analysis
This document demonstrates:
1. **Integrated sales system** - Shopify to SAGE automation
2. **Clear paper trail** - Proforma → POP → Tax Invoice
3. **Multiple touchpoints** - Kent and EL as key personnel
4. **Third-party verification** - Courier Guy tracking

### Potential Issues Identified
1. **Dual personnel control** - Both Kent and EL handle orders/POP
2. **Automation gap** - Manual POP receipt before fulfillment
3. **Revenue recognition timing** - Between proforma and tax invoice

### Relevance to Legal Filings
- Companies Act: Demonstrates operational structure
- POPIA: Shows customer data handling (email, phone, delivery)
- Tax Fraud: Shows tax invoice generation process
- Commercial Crime: Establishes revenue flow documentation
