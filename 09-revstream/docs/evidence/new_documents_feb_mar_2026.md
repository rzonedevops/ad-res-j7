## ND-01: Entity Answering Affidavit — Void Ab Initio

**Date:** 2026-02-19 | **Deponent:** Third to Sixth Respondents (via Daniel James Faucitt)

This is the central defense document. It argues that the ex parte interdict granted on 19 August 2025 is **void ab initio** (void from inception) because the founding affidavit contained deliberate falsehoods and material non-disclosures.

### Core Arguments

The affidavit establishes four pillars that render the interdict void:

| Pillar | Argument | Evidence |
|--------|----------|----------|
| **SOLE Mandate** | The FNB banking mandate was "SOLE," meaning each director could transact independently. No authorization was required for the R500,000 transfer. | JF-BS1 (FICA/KYC), EA-06 (FNB Legal letter) |
| **Reimbursement, Not Gift** | The R500,000 was a partial reimbursement for business expenses incurred by Daniel, not an unauthorized "birthday gift" as claimed. | Bank records, expense documentation |
| **Perjury with Foreknowledge** | The Applicant had documentary proof (FNB Legal letter dated 18 June 2025) contradicting his sworn statements before filing the interdict on 14 August 2025. | EA-06, founding affidavit comparison |
| **Digital Impersonation** | Peter Faucitt does not use electronic communication systems. All digital actions attributed to him were performed by Rynette Farrar, his agent. | Email headers, system access logs |
