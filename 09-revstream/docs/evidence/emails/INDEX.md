## Notes

- **Duplicates:** Many emails appear twice because they were synced from multiple Exchange mailboxes (e.g., pete@regima.zone and dan@regima.zone). Both copies are preserved for completeness.
- **Empty Body Content:** Some emails (particularly Lead 1a #2, Lead 1b #7, and Lead 1c) have empty body_content in the database. This typically means the body was not synced during the initial delta sync. The body_preview field (visible in the email headers) still contains the key content.
- **Attachments:** Several emails have `has_attachments: true`. The attachment files themselves are stored in the `exchange_sync.attachments` table and can be downloaded separately.
