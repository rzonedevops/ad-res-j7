# Stylometric Analysis Key Findings

## The "Pete" vs "Peter" Distinction

- **Jacqui** (wife) calls him **"Pete"** — familiar, intimate
- **Rynette** (bookkeeper) calls him **"Peter"** — formal, professional

## Four Distinct Phases

### Phase 1: Pre-Rynette (2018–mid 2020) — JACQUI + PETER HIMSELF
- 2018: 100% "Peter" sign-offs (45.5%) — this is actually Peter dictating to Jacqui, and he refers to himself as "Peter"
- 2019: Mixed — low volume, some "Pete" appears in Aug-Sep 2019
- Early 2020: Transition begins — "Pete" starts appearing as Jacqui takes over more typing

### Phase 2: Jacqui Dominant (mid 2020–mid 2023) — JACQUI
- "Pete" sign-off dominates: 23-44% vs "Peter" at 0-8%
- Volume explodes from ~10/month to 90-170/month
- "Kind Regards" appears (51 in 2021!) — Jacqui's formal closing
- "Cheers" appears occasionally — Jacqui's casual closing

### Phase 3: Transition (mid 2023–early 2025) — MIXED/RYNETTE INCREASING
- After Kayla dies (Jul 2023), "Peter" sign-offs surge
- Oct 2023: "Peter" jumps to 20.2% (from near 0%)
- Nov 2023: "Peter" overtakes "Pete" for first time (28.8% vs 19.2%)
- Dec 2023: Dead even (28% vs 28%)
- 2024: Both low but "Peter" slightly higher — Rynette gaining control
- "Kindly" appears (Rynette's word) — 4 in Oct 2023, then recurring

### Phase 4: Rynette Full Control (Mar 2025–Jul 2025) — RYNETTE
- Mar 2025: "Peter" explodes to 32.0% vs "Pete" at 3.3%
- Apr 2025: "Peter" at 77.9% — near total Rynette control
- May 2025: "Peter" at 65.5%
- Jun 2025: "Peter" at 64.5%
- Jul 2025: Last month — "Peter" at 38.5% (only 13 messages before blocked)

## Critical Insight: The 2018 "Peter" Messages
The 2018 messages show "Peter" sign-offs — but this is NOT Rynette (she hadn't joined yet). 
This is actually Peter dictating to Jacqui, and he naturally refers to himself as "Peter" (formal).
When Jacqui writes independently (not from dictation), she uses "Pete" — her familiar name for him.
This actually STRENGTHENS the analysis: "Peter" = either Peter himself dictating OR Rynette writing.
