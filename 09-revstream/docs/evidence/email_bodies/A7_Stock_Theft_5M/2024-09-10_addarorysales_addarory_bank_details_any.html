Set up a direct bank feed into Sage Accounting

Dear Valued Customer

Did you know that if you are banked with FNB, you have the convenience of a direct feed from your FNB Business Account into your Sage Accounting product? At no extra cost to you!

So what does this mean for you?
No more .csv files or third-party interventions - you can safely and securely set up an automated bank feed via FNB Online Banking and your Sage product, to automatically consolidate your financial transactions.

For a more detailed guide click here.

You’re just a few simple steps away

Step 1: Log into your Sage Accounting application and search for your bank under the “Connect your Bank” menu.
Step 2: Click the tick box to agree to the bank terms and conditions to go to the authorization screen, then select log in or register.
Step 3:  Select the account(s) you would like to link, agree to the terms and conditions, and click on “Authorize”. You will then be redirected back to Sage

Set up a quicker, more secure and efficient direct bank feed today.
