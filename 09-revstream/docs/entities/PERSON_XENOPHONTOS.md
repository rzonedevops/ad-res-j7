## Role in the Conspiracy

Nicos Xenophontos is Peter Faucitt's personal attorney who acted in two critical, interconnected capacities that bridge the Sage system sabotage and the Ketoni payout motive.

### 1. The Sage Sabotage (8 July 2024)
Xenophontos acted as the Commissioner of Oaths who commissioned Peter Faucitt's sworn affidavit to take over the Sage Business Cloud Accounting system. 
- He witnessed Peter swearing an oath to take over a cloud computing system (contradicting Peter's later claim that he "does not use computers").
- He facilitated the legal instrument that allowed Peter and Rynette to revoke all other users' access and break the API integrations.

### 2. The Ketoni Payout (January 2025)
Xenophontos acted as Peter's legal advisor regarding the Ketoni Investment Holdings shareholders agreement (the source of the R18.75M payout).
- On 24 January 2025, he emailed Peter requesting "missing pages" of the agreement to "advise you accordingly."
- This proves Xenophontos had direct knowledge of the Ketoni financial structure and the stakes involved.

## Provable Foreknowledge

Xenophontos had provable foreknowledge of both the **means** (the Sage system takeover) and the **motive** (the Ketoni R18.75M payout). If he was involved in drafting or reviewing the founding affidavit for the August 2025 interdict, he would have known that Peter's claims about Daniel failing to produce invoices were fraudulent, because Xenophontos himself commissioned the affidavit that enabled Peter to make those invoices inaccessible.
