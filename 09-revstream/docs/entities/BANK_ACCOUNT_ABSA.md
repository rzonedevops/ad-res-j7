# BANK_ACCOUNT_ABSA: Suspected Fraudulent ABSA Accounts

**Type:** Bank Account

## Role in Case

This entity represents the eight suspected fraudulent ABSA bank accounts that were allegedly opened by Rynette Farrar using a stolen ID document. These accounts are believed to have been used to divert funds from the RegimA group of companies.

## Significance

The creation and use of these accounts are central to the allegations of fraud, theft, and money laundering. Tracing the funds through these accounts is a critical part of the forensic investigation.

## Evidence

- Allegations made in correspondence and legal filings.
- Analysis of financial records showing unexplained outflows.
- Forensic investigation into banking records is required to confirm the existence and ownership of these accounts.
