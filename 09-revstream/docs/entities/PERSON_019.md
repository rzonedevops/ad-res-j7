## Role in Case

Oliver, also referred to as "Arthur," is an employee of the RegimA group entities. He performs dual functions:

1. **Business services**: Warehouse operations, stock management, stock removals, and stock taking
2. **Household services**: Garden services and property maintenance at 20 River Road, Morning Hill

Oliver is paid by the entities (not by Jacqueline personally), making him a company employee rather than a personal employee of any individual.

## Legal Significance

Oliver is a key witness to the contempt of court conduct. The removal of his work phone constitutes interference with business operations in breach of the court order. His testimony regarding the stock-blocking incident on 10 January 2026 supports the contempt application.

The Elliott Attorneys letter of 2 December 2025 (PF17) specifically demanded the return of Oliver's company phone and reminded Jacqueline that Oliver is an employee of the entities.
