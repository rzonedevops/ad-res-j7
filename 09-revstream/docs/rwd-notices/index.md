# RWD Formal Notices (February 2026)

**Last Updated:** 2026-02-14

This section contains formal corporate notices issued by Regima Worldwide Distribution (Pty) Ltd (RWD), acting through a majority of its Board of Directors. These notices address critical governance concerns, material non-disclosure in legal proceedings, and the systematic destruction of the company's operational infrastructure.

These documents are not court filings but are formal corporate communications placing all stakeholders on notice of documentary evidence that has come to the Board's attention.

## Key Themes and Allegations

1.  **Identity Misappropriation:** The notices allege that the identity and authority of director Mr. Peter Andrew Faucitt have been systematically misappropriated by third parties, specifically Ms. Rynette Farrar, who operated his email accounts and instructed legal and financial service providers on his behalf.

2.  **Material Non-Disclosure:** RWD/NOTICE/001 argues that the ex parte interdict of 19 August 2025 was obtained by withholding critical evidence from the court, including FNB's own records confirming a SOLE banking mandate that contradicted the Applicant's claims. This, the notice argues, renders the interdict **void ab initio**.

3.  **Operational Sabotage:** RWD/NOTICE/002 details the systematic destruction of the company's integrated e-commerce fulfillment pipeline, including the decommissioning of Shopify, the breaking of the Courier Guy API, and the concentration of all revenue into an opaque, single-user-controlled Sage Pastel system.

4.  **Provable Knowledge:** The notices place all parties (including attorneys, forensic investigators, and banks) on notice of the documentary evidence, stating that any continued action under the authority of the impugned court order is done with provable knowledge of the fraud and at their own risk.
