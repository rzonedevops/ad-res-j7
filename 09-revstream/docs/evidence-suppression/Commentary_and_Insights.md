## The Core Narrative: Concealment and Intimidation

The central theme is the attempt to use legal processes—settlement agreements, urgent applications, and even criminal complaints—not for their intended purpose, but as tools to suppress evidence and intimidate witnesses. The timeline of events is critical to understanding this narrative.

### 1. The "Settlement" That Wasn't a Settlement (September 2025)

The **Notice of Withdrawal (AF3)** [1] is the smoking gun that exposes the initial attempt at evidence suppression. The settlement agreements of 18 September 2025 were not a genuine attempt to resolve a dispute. As outlined in the withdrawal notice, they were designed to:

*   **Prevent the reporting of crimes:** The agreements sought to bind Jacqueline and Daniel Faucitt to silence, preventing them from reporting the financial irregularities they had uncovered.
*   **Suppress evidence:** The agreements were an attempt to bury the evidence of financial misconduct, including the **R12.3 million transfer** [2] that occurred just seven days before mediation.
*   **Circumvent legal obligations:** The agreements were a tool to avoid legal and regulatory obligations, such as those under POPIA.

The withdrawal from these agreements was a pivotal moment, as it signaled that the attempt to suppress evidence had failed.

### 2. The Urgent Application: A Weapon of Intimidation (November 2025)

Peter Faucitt's **Urgent Application (KF0019)** [3] was the next phase of the evidence suppression strategy. This 151-page document, filed on 3 November 2025, was not a genuine attempt to seek urgent relief, but rather a tool to intimidate and overwhelm the respondents. The application is replete with allegations that are contradicted by the very evidence submitted.

#### Rynette Farrar's Confirmatory Affidavit (AF26)

The most critical piece of evidence in this application is **Rynette Farrar's Confirmatory Affidavit (AF26)** [4]. In this sworn statement, Rynette confirms that Peter Faucitt's allegations about her are "both true and correct." This is a devastating admission, as it directly contradicts her later actions.

### 3. The Perjury Complaint: A Desperate Act of Obstruction (Post-November 2025)

After Jacqueline Faucitt filed her **Answering Affidavit** [5], which repeated the same allegations that Rynette had already confirmed as true, Rynette filed a perjury complaint against Jacqueline. This act is the most blatant example of evidence suppression in this case.

The **Evidence Matrix (SF15)** [6] systematically dismantles this perjury complaint, proving that every allegation made by Jacqueline is supported by documentary evidence. The perjury complaint is, therefore, a false criminal report and an act of **obstruction of justice**.
