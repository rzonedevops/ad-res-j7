**Last Updated:** 2026-03-09

## Description

This relation documents the insider trading network surrounding the Ketoni Investment Holdings ZAR 18.685M payout to the Faucitt Family Trust. Daniel Jacobus Bantjies occupies a unique position as both CFO of the George Group (Ketoni's parent) and Trustee of the FFT (the creditor), creating an irreconcilable conflict of interest that he exploited to facilitate the seizure of the payout.
