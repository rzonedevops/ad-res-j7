# MINUTES OF A MEETING OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS

**COMPANY:** Regima Worldwide Distribution (Pty) Ltd (2011/005722/07)

**DATE:** 16 February 2026

**PRESENT:**
- Mrs. Jacqueline Faucitt (Director)
- Mr. Daniel James Faucitt (Director)

**APOLOGIES:**
- Mr. Peter Andrew Faucitt (Director)

---

### 1. OPENING & QUORUM

A meeting of the Board of Directors was held. A quorum was present and the meeting was duly constituted.

### 2. SUBJECT OF THE MEETING

The purpose of the meeting was to consider and vote on a resolution for the removal of Mr. Peter Andrew Faucitt as a director of the Company in terms of Section 71(3) of the Companies Act 71 of 2008.

### 3. NOTICE & REPRESENTATIONS

It was noted that:
- Mr. Faucitt was given proper written notice of the proposed resolution and the grounds for his removal, in accordance with Section 71(4) of the Act.
- Mr. Faucitt was afforded a reasonable opportunity to make representations but declined to do so.

### 4. CONSIDERATION OF EVIDENCE

The Board considered the substantial evidence presented, including the contents of notices RWD/NOTICE/001 and RWD/NOTICE/002, which detail Mr. Faucitt's dereliction of duties, including fraud on the court and operational sabotage.

### 5. RESOLUTION

The following resolution was put to a vote:

> *"That the Board of Directors determines, in terms of Section 71(3)(b) of the Companies Act, that Director Peter Andrew Faucitt has neglected and been derelict in the performance of the functions of a director, and is hereby removed from the office of director of Regima Worldwide Distribution (Pty) Ltd and all its subsidiary and related entities with immediate effect."*

### 6. VOTING

The resolution was passed by a majority vote:

- **IN FAVOUR:** Mrs. Jacqueline Faucitt, Mr. Daniel James Faucitt
- **AGAINST:** None

### 7. CLOSING

There being no further business, the meeting was closed.
