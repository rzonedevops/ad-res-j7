# Board Resolutions — Regima Worldwide Distribution (Pty) Ltd

## Overview

This section contains the formal board resolution package for the removal of Director Peter Andrew Faucitt under **Section 71(3)** of the Companies Act 71 of 2008, on the grounds of neglect and dereliction of the functions of a director.

## Legal Framework

Section 71(3) of the Companies Act permits the board of directors (excluding the director concerned) to remove a director who has been determined to be ineligible, disqualified, incapacitated, or who has **neglected or been derelict** in the performance of their functions. The company must have more than two directors (RWD has three), and the director must be given written notice and a reasonable opportunity to make representations before the vote (s71(4)).

## Process Flow

1. **Serve Notice** — Deliver the Notice to Director to Peter Faucitt (in person, registered mail, or email). Record proof of delivery.
2. **Wait for Response** — Allow 5 business days for Peter to indicate whether he wishes to make representations.
3. **Hold Board Meeting** — If no response, or after representations are heard, convene the board meeting and vote.
4. **Sign Resolution & Minutes** — Both Jacqui and Daniel sign the resolution and minutes.
5. **File CoR39** — Submit to CIPC with all supporting documents.
6. **Peter's Right of Review** — Note that under s71(5), Peter may apply to court within 20 business days to review the board's determination.

## Grounds Summary

The three grounds for the determination of neglect and dereliction are:

1. **Fraud on the Court** — Swearing a false affidavit to obtain an ex parte interdict with provable foreknowledge that the statements were untrue.
2. **Systematic Operational Sabotage** — Overseeing the deliberate destruction of the Company's revenue pipeline (Shopify, Courier Guy API, bank cards).
3. **Abdication of Duty** — Allowing an unauthorised third party to misappropriate his identity and act on his behalf in all company matters.
