# RESOLUTION OF THE BOARD OF DIRECTORS

**COMPANY:** Regima Worldwide Distribution (Pty) Ltd (2011/005722/07)

**DATE:** 16 February 2026

**SUBJECT: REMOVAL OF DIRECTOR PETER ANDREW FAUCITT (ID: 520430 5708 18 5) IN TERMS OF SECTION 71(3) OF THE COMPANIES ACT 71 OF 2008**

---

### PREAMBLE

1.  The Board of Directors of Regima Worldwide Distribution (Pty) Ltd (“the Company”) has considered substantial and irrefutable evidence concerning the conduct of Director Peter Andrew Faucitt.
2.  This evidence, detailed in the annexed notices RWD/NOTICE/001 and RWD/NOTICE/002, demonstrates that Mr. Faucitt has become ineligible to serve as a director and has been derelict in the performance of his functions.
3.  In accordance with Section 71(4) of the Companies Act 71 of 2008 (“the Act”), Mr. Faucitt was given written notice of the proposed resolution and the grounds for his removal, and afforded a reasonable opportunity to make representations.

### RESOLUTION

It is hereby resolved by a majority of the Board of Directors of the Company that:

1.  **DETERMINATION OF GROUNDS FOR REMOVAL:** The Board determines, in terms of Section 71(3)(b) of the Act, that Director Peter Andrew Faucitt has **neglected and been derelict in the performance of the functions of a director**.

    *   **Grounds for Determination:**
        *   **Fraud on the Court:** Knowingly providing false and misleading information under oath to the High Court to obtain an ex parte interdict on 19 August 2025, constituting a gross abuse of his position and a breach of his fiduciary duties.
        *   **Systematic Operational Sabotage:** Overseeing the deliberate dismantling of the Company’s core revenue-generating infrastructure, including the decommissioning of the Shopify e-commerce platform and the destruction of the automated fulfillment pipeline, thereby intentionally inflicting harm upon the Company.
        *   **Abdication of Duty:** Failing to act in the best interests of the Company by allowing an unauthorised third party, Ms. Rynette Farrar, to misappropriate his identity and act on his behalf in all company matters, including the fraudulent transfer of critical financial systems.

2.  **REMOVAL OF DIRECTOR:** In light of the above determination, Director Peter Andrew Faucitt is hereby **removed from the office of director** of Regima Worldwide Distribution (Pty) Ltd, and all its subsidiary and related entities, with immediate effect.

3.  **CIPC FILING:** The Company Secretary is hereby authorised and instructed to file the requisite CoR39 form and all supporting documentation with the Companies and Intellectual Property Commission (CIPC) to record the removal of Mr. Faucitt as a director.

4.  **AUTHORITY TO ACT:** Mrs. Jacqueline Faucitt and Mr. Daniel James Faucitt are authorised to sign all necessary documents and take all necessary steps to give effect to this resolution.
