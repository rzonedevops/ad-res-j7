This super-refined complaint details systematic and conclusive breaches of the Companies Act, No. 71 of 2008, and the Trust Property Control Act, No. 57 of 1988, by Director Peter Andrew Faucitt and a syndicate of co-conspirators. Key evidence includes the **Shopify centrality organogram** (proving premeditation), **fabricated 2019 financial statements** (tax fraud), bank statements showing **99.8% asset stripping**, Peter Faucitt's own **FNB Fraud Letter (SF10)** (direct admission of fraud awareness), **Sage accounting records (SF10/SF11)** proving Rynette Farrar’s identity fraud and system control, and the **Perjury Defence Matrix (SF15)**, which proves a subsequent attempt to obstruct justice. The evidence presented meets and exceeds the standards for both civil and criminal liability, mandating a Section 162 Director Disqualification application and referral for criminal prosecution.
