## 4. The SARS Query — The Smoking Gun (June 2025)

### What Happened

On **5 June 2025**, Rynette emailed Bantjes with subject "SARS query re the two big inter company invoices at the end of February 2025":

> "Anton forwarded the letter via WhatsApp to Pete's phone. I do not know where this letter was sent to, but it is not on my Efiling portal, and it did not come to any of our emails."

> "This seems to be questions re **the two big inter company invoices that you have pushed through at the end of February**."

> "Can you give us the explanation to SARS in writing so that we can respond?"

On **6 June 2025**, Rynette clarified:

> "**The two big invoices were done on your request, for which I have no answer.**"

> "Are you going to answer this, or do you want to give me the answers to the questions relating to the two invoices, and I will reply to SARS?"

Bantjes then drafted a response to SARS, with multiple revisions between 8-9 June 2025. SARS issued a second notice requesting different documentation. By **30 June 2025**, Rynette confirmed she had uploaded all documentation.

### Why It Matters

1. **Bantjes pushed through the invoices**: Rynette explicitly states "the two big inter company invoices that **you** have pushed through" — Bantjes directed these transactions
2. **Rynette had no explanation**: The bookkeeper who processed the invoices says she has "no answer" for what they are — meaning Bantjes instructed her to process invoices she didn't understand
3. **SARS flagged them**: The South African Revenue Service independently identified these as suspicious — they were large enough to trigger automated or manual review
4. **Timing**: End of February 2025 — the financial year-end — is when these large invoices were pushed through, suggesting they were designed to manipulate the year-end financial position
5. **The SARS letter was hidden**: It wasn't on Rynette's eFiling portal and didn't come to any company emails — it was forwarded via WhatsApp by "Anton" to Pete's phone, suggesting deliberate concealment
6. **Bantjes drafted the response**: The accountant who created the invoices also drafted the explanation to SARS — a clear conflict of interest
