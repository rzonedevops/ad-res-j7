## TRANSCRIPTION NOTES

This document is the **Confirmatory Affidavit** of Daniel Jacobus Bantjes, sworn on **13 August 2025** at **Pretoria** before Commissioner of Oaths **Hannelle Evert** (SAIBA16721).

### Key Observations

1. **Deponent Identity:** Daniel Jacobus Bantjes, ID 581004 5103 089, SAICA member 00105928.

2. **Dual Role Disclosure:** Paragraph 1.2 reveals Bantjes serves as accountant for **both** the applicant (Peter Faucitt) **and** the 3rd to 6th respondents (RegimA Worldwide Distribution, RegimA Skin Treatments CC, Villa Via Arcadia No 2 CC, Strategic Logistics CC). This is a significant conflict of interest — he is the accountant for both sides of the dispute.

3. **Blanket Confirmation:** Paragraph 2.1 contains a blanket confirmation of Peter Faucitt's founding affidavit, qualified only by "as they may pertain to me." This is a remarkably broad endorsement given the serious allegations in the founding affidavit.

4. **Brevity:** The entire substantive content of the affidavit is only 5 paragraphs across 2 pages. For a confirmatory affidavit supporting an ex parte application seeking sweeping interdictory relief, this is unusually brief and lacks any independent factual content.

5. **Commissioner:** Hannelle Evert is a Chartered Business Accountant in Practice (SA), member SAIBA16721, operating from Libertas Office Park, Block E, Equestria 0184. She is **not** a legal practitioner but a fellow accountant — the affidavit was commissioned at what appears to be Bantjes' own office or a neighbouring office in the same business park.

6. **Location:** Sworn at Pretoria (Equestria), not at a police station or attorney's office.

### Evidentiary Significance for Void Ab Initio

This affidavit is a **critical pillar** of the void ab initio analysis:

1. **Perjury with Foreknowledge:** Bantjes had received Daniel Faucitt's fraud report 68 days before swearing this affidavit. He knew the allegations in Peter's founding affidavit were false, yet confirmed them under oath.

2. **Undisclosed Conflict of Interest:** As accountant for both the applicant and the respondent companies, and as a trustee connected to the R18.685M Ketoni payout, Bantjes had material conflicts that were not disclosed to the court.

3. **No Independent Facts:** The affidavit contains zero independent factual assertions. It is a pure rubber-stamp of Peter's narrative, despite Bantjes having personal knowledge that contradicted it.
