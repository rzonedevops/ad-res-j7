## Key Financial Evidence

The combined technology and platform spend documented in PF9 and PF10 totals approximately **R8.85 million** (R6,738,007 + R2,116,159.47). This represents the legitimate business expenditure that Peter Faucitt characterised as unauthorised in his founding affidavit, despite these being standard operational costs for the e-commerce platforms built and maintained by Daniel Faucitt.

## Void Ab Initio Relevance

This document is the primary subject of the void ab initio analysis. The founding affidavit contains material non-disclosures and statements contradicted by the FNB SOLE mandate evidence (received 18 June 2025, two months before the affidavit was sworn on 13 August 2025). The confirmatory affidavit of Bantjes is similarly compromised, as Bantjes received Daniel's fraud report on 6 June 2025 yet certified the affidavit on 13 August 2025.
