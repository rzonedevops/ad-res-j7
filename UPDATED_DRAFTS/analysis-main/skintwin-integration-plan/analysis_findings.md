## Core Problem Statement
RegimA Medic project was halted due to inability to scale hardware-based diagnostic equipment:
- Hardware diagnostic equipment (Visia™ or Antera 3D™) costs 6+ figures per unit
- Network of ~1,200 salons/clinics with ~4,000 therapists in South Africa
- Deploying to <5% of outlets would cost more than building custom hardware
- Hardware requires co-location, limiting scalability
