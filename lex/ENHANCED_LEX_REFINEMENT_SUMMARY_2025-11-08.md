### 2.1 Legal Framework

**Rule 42(1)(a) - Uniform Rules of Court:**
> "The court may, in addition to any other powers it may have, motu proprio or on the application of any party affected, rescind or vary an order or judgment erroneously sought or erroneously granted in the absence of any party affected thereby."

**Grounds for Rescission:**
1. ✓ **Erroneously sought:** Application based on false/misleading information
2. ✓ **Erroneously granted:** Court misled by material non-disclosures
3. ✓ **In absence of party:** Ex parte application without notice
4. ✓ **Fraud:** Order obtained through perjury and material non-disclosure
