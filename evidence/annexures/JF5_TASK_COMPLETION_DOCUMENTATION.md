## Key Insights from Framework

### Evidentiary Strategy
The framework recognizes that mediation agreement restrictions prevent use of draft copies as evidence. Therefore, it focuses on:

1. **Provable from Final Agreement Alone:**
   - Incoherent terminology (5 undefined professional categories)
   - Duplicate cost clauses (2.6 and Section 3)
   - Missing selection procedures
   - Unlimited financial scope
   - No cost caps

2. **Legal Arguments Supported:**
   - Unconscionability (procedural + substantive)
   - Lack of meeting of minds
   - Incomplete agreement

3. **Strategic Connections:**
   - 8-day gap between settlement and interdict filing
   - "Has anything changed?" exchange timing
   - Investment payout motive (9-month timeline)

### Legal Attention Mechanism
The framework includes multi-head attention analysis:
- **Causal Head**: Cause-effect chains between changes
- **Intentionality Head**: Mental states and strategic intent
- **Temporal Head**: Timing relative to litigation filing
- **Normative Head**: Rule violations and legal breaches

This enables quantitative ranking of change significance.
