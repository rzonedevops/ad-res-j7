## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

This annexure contains financial management system access control logs, banking interface audit trails, and accounting system permission records demonstrating that Peter Faucitt completely locked Daniel Faucitt out of all financial systems immediately after Daniel provided comprehensive reports to the accountant. This evidence establishes that Peter systematically prevented access to the financial records he later demanded as "missing documentation."

1. **Financial Management System Logs** - Complete access revocation to financial databases
2. **Banking Interface Access Records** - Denial of access to transaction histories and statements
3. **Director Loan Account System Logs** - Blocking of access to director loan account records
4. **Accounting Software Audit Trails** - Permission downgrades and system lockouts
5. **Payment Processing System Logs** - Restriction of access to vendor payment records

## LEGAL FRAMEWORK

### Admissibility of Financial System Logs:

#### 1. Electronic Evidence Standards

**Electronic Communications and Transactions Act 25 of 2002:**
- **Section 15(1):** Financial system logs admissible as data messages
- **Section 15(2):** Court considers reliability of system maintenance
- **Section 15(3):** Integrity presumed if proper authentication provided
- **Section 15(4):** Data message best evidence of information it contains

**Authentication Methods:**
- System-generated logs (automatic audit trail)
- Accountant certification of export authenticity
- IT professional affidavit confirming log integrity
- Software vendor documentation of audit trail reliability

#### 2. Business Records Hearsay Exception

**Law of Evidence Amendment Act 45 of 1988:**
- Financial audit logs created in ordinary course of business
- Automatic generation by accounting software without human bias
- Regular business practice to maintain audit trails for compliance
- Logs created contemporaneously with user actions

#### 3. Financial Records Standards

**Accounting Standards and Compliance:**
- IFRS requirements for audit trail maintenance
- SARS (South African Revenue Service) compliance requirements
- Companies Act audit trail obligations
- Close Corporations Act financial record keeping
